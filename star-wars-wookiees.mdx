# Wookiees

Wookiees are a tall, powerful species native to the forest world of Kashyyyk. They are renowned for their strength and loyalty.

## Life debts

A Wookiee who is saved may swear a life debt, a lifelong bond of loyalty that they honor above nearly all else.

## Kashyyyk

The Wookiee homeworld is covered in colossal wroshyr trees, with cities built high among the branches.
