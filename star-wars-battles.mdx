## Overview

The Star Wars saga is shaped by its battles. From sweeping space engagements to ground assaults on distant worlds, these conflicts define the rise and fall of the Republic, Empire, and New Republic.

## Clone Wars

The Clone Wars pitted the Galactic Republic against the Confederacy of Independent Systems.

- **Battle of Geonosis** — The opening engagement of the war, where the Grand Army of the Republic was first deployed.
- **Battle of Christophsis** — An early Republic victory featuring Anakin Skywalker and his new Padawan, Ahsoka Tano.
- **Battle of Coruscant** — A massive space battle above the Republic capital that led to the rescue of Chancellor Palpatine.

## Galactic Civil War

The struggle between the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance produced some of the saga's most iconic moments.

- **Battle of Scarif** — The Rebels stole the Death Star plans, igniting open war.
- **Battle of Yavin** — Luke Skywalker destroyed the first Death Star.
- **Battle of Hoth** — The Empire overran the Rebel base on the ice planet Hoth.
- **Battle of Endor** — The Rebel Alliance destroyed the second Death Star and Emperor Palpatine.

## First Order conflict

Decades after Endor, the Resistance fought the First Order and the resurrected Sith Eternal.

- **Attack on Starkiller Base** — The Resistance destroyed the First Order's superweapon.
- **Battle of Crait** — A desperate Resistance stand on a remote mineral world.
- **Battle of Exegol** — The final confrontation that ended the Sith threat.
