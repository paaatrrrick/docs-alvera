## Overview

From battle-scarred freighters to planet-killing superweapons, the vehicles of Star Wars are as iconic as its characters. This page covers the most notable ships and vehicles across the saga.

## Hero ships

### Millennium Falcon

A modified YT-1300 light freighter famously piloted by Han Solo and Chewbacca. Known for completing the Kessel Run in less than twelve parsecs, the Falcon appears across the original, sequel, and standalone films.

### X-wing starfighter

The signature snub fighter of the Rebel Alliance and Resistance. Its S-foils lock into attack position to form the iconic "X" shape. Luke Skywalker flew an X-wing during the Battle of Yavin.

### Slave I / Boba Fett's starship

A Firespray-class patrol craft used by the bounty hunters Jango and Boba Fett. It flies in a vertical orientation in atmosphere and lands horizontally.

## Ground and specialty vehicles

- **AT-AT walker**: Four-legged Imperial assault walker used at the Battle of Hoth.
- **Speeder bike**: High-speed repulsorlift vehicle, famously used during the chase on Endor.
- **Sandcrawler**: Massive treaded transport operated by Jawas on Tatooine.
- **Podracer**: High-speed racing craft seen on Tatooine in *The Phantom Menace*.

## Jedi and Republic craft

- **Jedi starfighter**: Compact fighters such as the Delta-7 *Aethersprite* and Eta-2 *Actis* flown by Jedi during the Clone Wars.
- **Venator-class Star Destroyer**: Republic capital ship that served as the precursor to the Imperial-class.
- **Naboo Royal Starship**: Sleek chrome-hulled diplomatic cruiser used by Queen Amidala.
