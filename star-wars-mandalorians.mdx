# Mandalorians

Mandalorians are a clan-based people defined by a warrior culture that spans many species. Their distinctive armor, often forged from beskar steel, is passed down through generations.

## The Creed

Many Mandalorians follow a strict code known as the Way, which governs honor, combat, and the wearing of the helmet. Foundlings are taken in and raised as members of the clan.

## Beskar

Beskar is a rare metal capable of deflecting blaster fire and resisting lightsaber blades, making it prized for armor across the galaxy.
