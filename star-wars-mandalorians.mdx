# Mandalorians

The Mandalorians are a clan-based warrior culture from the planet Mandalore. They are renowned across the galaxy for their beskar armor, strict code of honor, and long history of conflict with the Jedi Order.

## The Way

Followers of the traditional Creed live by a strict code, including the tenet "This is the Way." Members never remove their helmets in the presence of others.

## Beskar armor

Beskar is a near-indestructible iron mined on Mandalore. Forged into armor and weapons, it can withstand lightsaber strikes and blaster fire.

## Notable Mandalorians

- **Din Djarin** — A bounty hunter of the Children of the Watch who became the foundling guardian of Grogu.
- **Bo-Katan Kryze** — Wielder of the Darksaber and heir to House Kryze.
- **Boba Fett** — The cloned son of Jango Fett and former Daimyo of Tatooine.
- **Sabine Wren** — Artist, warrior, and member of the Ghost crew.

## The Darksaber

A unique black-bladed lightsaber created by Tarre Vizsla, the first Mandalorian Jedi. Possession of the Darksaber is traditionally tied to leadership of Mandalore.
