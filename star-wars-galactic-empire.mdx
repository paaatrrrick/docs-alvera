# The Galactic Empire

The Galactic Empire rose from the ashes of the Republic, ushering in an era of central control backed by overwhelming military force.

## Military might

The Empire projected power through massive fleets, legions of stormtroopers, and superweapons capable of destroying entire worlds.

## Rule by fear

Imperial governors maintained order through intimidation, dissolving local institutions and crushing dissent wherever it arose.
