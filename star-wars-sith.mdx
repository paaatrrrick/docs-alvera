## Overview

The Sith are an ancient order of Force-wielders who draw power from the dark side. Where the [Jedi](/star-wars-jedi-order) seek harmony and serve others, the Sith pursue passion, strength, and domination.

## The Sith Code

The Sith philosophy is summarized in their code:

> Peace is a lie, there is only passion.
> Through passion, I gain strength.
> Through strength, I gain power.
> Through power, I gain victory.
> Through victory, my chains are broken.
> The Force shall free me.

## The Rule of Two

After centuries of internal warfare nearly destroyed the order, Darth Bane established the Rule of Two: at any time there shall be only a master to embody power and an apprentice to crave it. This kept the Sith hidden for a thousand years before their return.

## Notable Sith Lords

| Sith Lord | Era | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Darth Bane | Old Republic | Founder of the Rule of Two |
| Darth Plagueis | Pre-prequels | Master of life and death; mentor to Sidious |
| Darth Sidious | Prequel and original eras | Emperor of the Galactic Empire |
| Darth Maul | Prequel era | Sidious's first apprentice |
| Count Dooku | Clone Wars | Leader of the Separatists |
| Darth Vader | Original trilogy | Sidious's enforcer; redeemed Anakin Skywalker |
| Kylo Ren | Sequel trilogy | Dark-side heir, ultimately redeemed |

## Light and dark

The Sith are inseparable from the broader story of the [Force](/star-wars-force) and stand in opposition to the [Jedi Order](/star-wars-jedi-order). Their conflicts shape the major [battles](/star-wars-battles) and [eras](/star-wars-eras) of the galaxy.
