This page explain how billing works so their is no surprise on you’re invoice.

## Plans and charges

You pick a plan and we bill you monthly or yearly. Yearly is cheaper, you saves about two month. See [the plans page](/pricing-overview) for the number.

Some feature is usage based, witch mean you pay for what you uses on top of you’re base plan. The [usage page](#usage-breakdwn) show this in real time.

## Payment methods

We accept card and, on bigger plan, invoicing. Add or update you’re card on [the billing settings](/settings/billing-info). If a payment fail, we retry it a few time before we suspend the account.

Got a question about a charge? [Reach out to billing support](https://billing.example-broken.dev/help) or read [the billing FAQ](./billing/faq-missing).
