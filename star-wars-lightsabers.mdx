## Overview

Lightsabers are the signature weapons of the Jedi and Sith, consisting of a plasma blade powered by a kyber crystal and emitted from a metal hilt. Elegant, precise, and deeply personal, each lightsaber is built by its wielder as a rite of passage.

## Construction

A lightsaber is assembled from a small number of essential components:

- **Kyber crystal** — a Force-attuned crystal that focuses the blade and determines its color.
- **Hilt** — the metal handle, customized by the wielder for grip, balance, and style.
- **Power cell** — supplies energy to the blade.
- **Emitter** — shapes and projects the plasma blade.
- **Activation switch** — ignites or retracts the blade.

## Blade colors

Blade color is determined by the wielder's bond with their kyber crystal.

| Color | Common meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Blue | Jedi Guardian, combat-focused |
| Green | Jedi Consular, wisdom-focused |
| Purple | Rare; balance between light and dark |
| Yellow | Jedi Temple Guard or Sentinel |
| Red | Sith; created by "bleeding" a kyber crystal |
| White | Purified crystal, freed from the dark side |

## Combat forms

The Jedi Order codified seven classical forms of lightsaber combat:

1. **Shii-Cho** — the foundational form.
2. **Makashi** — duelist's form, favored by Count Dooku.
3. **Soresu** — defensive form, mastered by Obi-Wan Kenobi.
4. **Ataru** — acrobatic, aggressive form, used by Yoda.
5. **Shien / Djem So** — counterattack form, used by Anakin Skywalker.
6. **Niman** — balanced, diplomatic form.
7. **Juyo / Vaapad** — ferocious form, mastered by Mace Windu.

## Notable lightsabers

- **The Skywalker saber** — built by Anakin, wielded by Luke and later Rey.
- **The Darksaber** — an ancient black-bladed saber tied to Mandalorian leadership.
- **Mace Windu's saber** — distinctive purple blade.
- **Darth Maul's saberstaff** — double-bladed weapon with a red blade at each end.
- **Kylo Ren's crossguard saber** — unstable red blade with lateral vents.
