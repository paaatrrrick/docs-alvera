## Overview

Lightsabers are the signature weapons of the Jedi and Sith. Each is hand-built by its wielder, powered by a kyber crystal, and considered as much a symbol of identity as a tool of combat.

## Construction

A lightsaber's core components include:

- **Kyber crystal**: A Force-attuned crystal that focuses the blade's energy and determines its color.
- **Hilt**: The metal handle, typically 24-30 cm long, housing the power cell and emitter.
- **Emitter**: Projects and contains the plasma blade.
- **Activation switch**: Ignites and deactivates the blade.

## Blade colors

| Color | Common wielders | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Blue | Jedi Guardians | Combat-focused Jedi |
| Green | Jedi Consulars | Wisdom and diplomacy |
| Purple | Mace Windu | Balance between light and dark |
| Yellow | Jedi Sentinels, Temple Guards | Defenders and investigators |
| Red | Sith | Synthetic crystal "bled" through the dark side |
| White | Ahsoka Tano | Purified crystals freed from the dark side |
| Darksaber (black) | Mandalorian leaders | Unique ancient blade forged by Tarre Vizsla |

## Hilt variations

- **Standard single-blade**: The most common form, used by most Jedi and Sith.
- **Double-bladed (saberstaff)**: Two blades from one hilt, famously wielded by Darth Maul.
- **Curved hilt**: Favored by duelists like Count Dooku for precision in Form II combat.
- **Crossguard**: Vented side emitters, as seen with Kylo Ren.
- **Lightwhip**: A flexible plasma lash used by rare practitioners.

## Notable wielders

- **Luke Skywalker**: Built a green-bladed saber after losing his father's blue one on Bespin.
- **Anakin Skywalker / Darth Vader**: Wielded a blue blade as a Jedi and a red blade as a Sith.
- **Obi-Wan Kenobi**: Known for his elegant blue-bladed saber and mastery of Form III.
- **Yoda**: Carried a short green blade suited to his stature and acrobatic style.
- **Darth Maul**: Wielded a red double-bladed saberstaff.
- **Ahsoka Tano**: Dual-wielded white blades after leaving the Jedi Order.

## Combat forms

Jedi and Sith train in seven classical lightsaber forms, each with different strengths:

1. **Shii-Cho** — The foundational form.
2. **Makashi** — Dueling and precision.
3. **Soresu** — Defensive mastery.
4. **Ataru** — Acrobatic offense.
5. **Shien / Djem So** — Power and counterattack.
6. **Niman** — Balanced, diplomatic style.
7. **Juyo / Vaapad** — Aggressive and emotionally charged.
