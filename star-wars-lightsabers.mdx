## Overview

The lightsaber is the iconic weapon of the Jedi and Sith. Described as "an elegant weapon for a more civilized age," it consists of a plasma blade powered by a kyber crystal and contained within a metal hilt.

## Construction

A lightsaber's core components include:

- **Hilt** — The metal handle, typically hand-crafted by its wielder.
- **Kyber crystal** — A Force-attuned crystal that focuses the blade's energy and determines its color.
- **Power cell** — Provides the energy that the crystal channels into a plasma blade.
- **Emitter** — Shapes and projects the blade.
- **Activation switch** — Ignites or deactivates the weapon.

Jedi traditionally construct their own lightsaber as part of their training, a rite that reflects their connection to the Force.

## Kyber crystals

Kyber crystals are rare, Force-sensitive crystals found on planets such as Ilum and Jedha. They are colorless until bonded with a wielder through the Force, at which point they take on a color that reflects the user.

Sith lightsabers are red because the Sith "bleed" their crystals, corrupting them through pain and rage.

## Blade colors

Common blade colors and their typical meanings:

| Color  | Association |
| ------ | ----------- |
| Blue   | Jedi Guardians — combat-focused Jedi |
| Green  | Jedi Consulars — Force scholars and diplomats |
| Yellow | Jedi Sentinels and Temple Guards |
| Purple | Rare; famously wielded by Mace Windu |
| Red    | Sith and dark side users |
| White  | Crystals purified from corruption, wielded by Ahsoka Tano |

## Forms of combat

Jedi and Sith study seven classical forms of lightsaber combat:

1. **Shii-Cho** — The foundational form.
2. **Makashi** — A dueling form favored by Count Dooku.
3. **Soresu** — A defensive form mastered by Obi-Wan Kenobi.
4. **Ataru** — An acrobatic, aggressive form used by Yoda.
5. **Shien / Djem So** — A counter-attacking form used by Anakin Skywalker.
6. **Niman** — A balanced, diplomatic form.
7. **Juyo / Vaapad** — An aggressive form channeling inner darkness, refined by Mace Windu.

## Notable lightsabers

- **Anakin Skywalker's blue saber** — Later wielded by Luke and Rey.
- **The Darksaber** — A unique black-bladed lightsaber tied to Mandalorian leadership.
- **Darth Maul's double-bladed saber** — A saberstaff with blades on both ends.
- **Kylo Ren's crossguard saber** — Built around a cracked, unstable crystal.
