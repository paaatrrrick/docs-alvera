## Overview

The lightsaber is the signature weapon of the Jedi and Sith — an elegant blade of pure plasma contained by a magnetic field, projected from a metal hilt. Wielding one well takes years of training, which is why it became a symbol of Force-sensitive warriors throughout the galaxy.

## Construction

A lightsaber is built around a few core components:

- **Hilt**: The metal handle that houses the internal components and provides grip.
- **Kyber crystal**: A Force-attuned crystal that focuses the energy into a contained blade. The crystal's bond with its wielder determines the blade's color.
- **Power cell**: Supplies the energy that the crystal channels.
- **Emitter matrix**: Shapes and projects the plasma blade.
- **Activation switch**: Ignites and retracts the blade.

Jedi traditionally build their own lightsaber as a rite of passage, often as part of the Gathering on Ilum.

## Blade colors

| Color | Typical wielder | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Blue | Jedi Guardian | Combat-focused Jedi |
| Green | Jedi Consular | Diplomacy and Force study |
| Purple | Mace Windu | Balance between light and dark |
| Yellow | Jedi Sentinel, Temple Guard | Investigation and protection |
| Red | Sith | Synthetic crystal "bled" through the dark side |
| White | Ahsoka Tano | A purified, formerly red crystal |

## Lightsaber forms

Jedi combat is organized into seven classical forms, each with its own emphasis:

1. **Shii-Cho** — the foundational form
2. **Makashi** — dueling and precision
3. **Soresu** — defensive resilience
4. **Ataru** — acrobatic offense
5. **Shien / Djem So** — power and counterattack
6. **Niman** — balanced, diplomatic style
7. **Juyo / Vaapad** — aggressive, emotionally charged combat

## Notable lightsabers

- **The Skywalker saber**: Built by Anakin Skywalker, later wielded by Luke and Rey.
- **Darth Vader's saber**: A red-bladed weapon constructed after his fall on Mustafar.
- **Yoda's saber**: A short-hilted green blade suited to his acrobatic style.
- **Mace Windu's saber**: The only known purple blade in the Jedi Order.
- **The Darksaber**: An ancient black-bladed weapon created by Tarre Vizsla, the first Mandalorian Jedi.
