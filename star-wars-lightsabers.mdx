## Overview

The lightsaber is the signature weapon of the Jedi and Sith. Each one is hand-built by its wielder as part of their training and is powered by a kyber crystal that determines the blade's color, sound, and behavior.

## Anatomy of a lightsaber

A standard lightsaber is built around a small number of core components:

- **Kyber crystal** — focuses the energy that forms the blade and bonds with its wielder.
- **Emitter** — projects the plasma blade from the hilt.
- **Power cell** — supplies energy to the crystal and emitter.
- **Hilt** — the grip and housing, often customized to reflect the builder.
- **Activation switch** — ignites and shuts down the blade.

## Blade colors

Blade color is determined by the kyber crystal and, in some traditions, the wielder's connection to the Force.

| Color | Common meaning | Notable wielders |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Blue | Jedi Guardian | Anakin Skywalker, Obi-Wan Kenobi, Luke Skywalker |
| Green | Jedi Consular | Yoda, Qui-Gon Jinn, Luke Skywalker |
| Yellow | Jedi Sentinel, Temple Guard | Rey, Jedi Temple Guards |
| Purple | Rare, balanced alignment | Mace Windu |
| Red | Synthetic crystal, "bled" by a Sith | Darth Vader, Darth Sidious, Kylo Ren |
| White | Purified crystal | Ahsoka Tano |
| Darksaber (black) | Ancient Mandalorian relic | Tarre Vizsla, Pre Vizsla, Bo-Katan Kryze |

## Common hilt designs

- **Single-blade** — the standard configuration used by most Jedi and Sith.
- **Double-bladed (saberstaff)** — a single hilt with an emitter on each end, used by Darth Maul.
- **Dual-wield** — two single hilts wielded together, as Ahsoka Tano often did.
- **Crossguard** — vented quillons form a cross, seen on Kylo Ren's saber.
- **Curved hilt** — angled grip favored by duelists like Count Dooku.

## Combat forms

Jedi and Sith study seven classical lightsaber forms, each suited to a different style of combat.

1. **Shii-Cho** — Form I, the foundational style.
2. **Makashi** — Form II, refined dueling, favored by Dooku.
3. **Soresu** — Form III, defensive, favored by Obi-Wan Kenobi.
4. **Ataru** — Form IV, acrobatic, favored by Yoda.
5. **Shien / Djem So** — Form V, counterattack, favored by Anakin Skywalker.
6. **Niman** — Form VI, balanced and diplomatic.
7. **Juyo / Vaapad** — Form VII, aggressive, mastered by Mace Windu.
