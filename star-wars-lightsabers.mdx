## Overview

The lightsaber is the signature weapon of the Jedi and Sith, an elegant blade of pure plasma held in place by a magnetic containment field. More than a weapon, a lightsaber is a symbol of its wielder's commitment to the Force and a reflection of their identity.

## Construction

Every lightsaber shares a core set of components, though hilts vary widely in style and material:

- **Kyber crystal.** The living heart of the weapon that focuses the blade's energy.
- **Emitter.** Shapes the plasma stream that becomes the blade.
- **Power cell.** Supplies the energy needed to ignite and sustain the blade.
- **Hilt.** The handle, often personalized to suit the wielder's grip and combat style.
- **Activation switch.** Ignites and deactivates the blade.

Jedi traditionally construct their own lightsabers as a rite of passage, meditating with a kyber crystal until it resonates with them.

## Blade colors

A lightsaber's color comes from the kyber crystal at its core. Common colors include:

- **Blue.** Wielded by Jedi Guardians who favor direct combat.
- **Green.** Favored by Jedi Consulars who focus on diplomacy and Force study.
- **Yellow.** Carried by the Jedi Temple Guards and Jedi Sentinels.
- **Purple.** A rare color associated with Mace Windu.
- **Red.** Forged by Sith who "bleed" a kyber crystal by pouring their rage into it.
- **White.** Purified crystals restored from Sith corruption, wielded by Ahsoka Tano.

## Combat forms

Jedi and Sith study seven classical forms of lightsaber combat, each suited to different situations:

| Form | Name | Focus |
| --- | --- | --- |
| I | Shii-Cho | The foundational form taught to all initiates. |
| II | Makashi | Precise dueling against a single opponent. |
| III | Soresu | Defensive combat and blaster deflection. |
| IV | Ataru | Acrobatic offense using speed and momentum. |
| V | Shien / Djem So | Counterattacks that redirect an opponent's power. |
| VI | Niman | A balanced form that pairs blade work with the Force. |
| VII | Juyo / Vaapad | An aggressive form that channels controlled fury. |

## Notable variants

- **Double-bladed lightsaber.** A single hilt that emits blades from both ends, famously used by Darth Maul.
- **Curved-hilt lightsaber.** A dueling hilt that improves grip angles, favored by Count Dooku.
- **Lightsaber pike.** An extended hilt designed for reach, carried by the Jedi Temple Guards.
- **Crossguard lightsaber.** An archaic design with vented side blades, wielded by Kylo Ren.
- **Darksaber.** A unique, black-bladed lightsaber that is a symbol of leadership among the Mandalorians.

## Cultural significance

To a Jedi, the lightsaber is a tool of defense and a reminder of their responsibility to protect life. To a Sith, it is an extension of their will and dominance. Losing one's lightsaber is considered a profound failure, and constructing a new one is an act of renewal.
