## Overview

The lightsaber is the signature weapon of the Jedi and Sith, described in *Star Wars* as "an elegant weapon for a more civilized age." Each lightsaber is hand-built by its wielder as part of their training, making every blade unique to the Force user who carries it.

## Construction

A lightsaber is built around a small set of core components housed in a metal hilt:

- **Kyber crystal** — a Force-attuned crystal that focuses the blade's energy.
- **Power cell** — the energy source that feeds the blade.
- **Emitter matrix** — shapes the plasma into a contained blade.
- **Activation stud** — ignites and extinguishes the weapon.
- **Hilt** — the grip, often personalized with materials and inlays meaningful to the builder.

## Kyber crystals

Kyber crystals are Force-sensitive crystals harvested from worlds such as Ilum and Jedha. Jedi younglings traditionally find their own crystal during a rite known as The Gathering. The crystal bonds to its user, and that bond determines the color of the blade.

Sith do not find crystals naturally — they take them by force and "bleed" them through the dark side, producing the signature red blade.

## Blade colors

| Color | Common meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Blue | Jedi Guardians, focused on combat |
| Green | Jedi Consulars, focused on diplomacy and the Force |
| Yellow | Jedi Temple Guards and Sentinels |
| Purple | Rare; famously wielded by Mace Windu |
| Red | Sith and dark side users, from bled crystals |
| White | Purified crystals, wielded by Ahsoka Tano |

## Lightsaber forms

Jedi developed seven classical forms of lightsaber combat, each suited to different opponents and philosophies:

1. **Shii-Cho** — the foundational form, taught to all younglings.
2. **Makashi** — elegant dueling form favored by Count Dooku.
3. **Soresu** — defensive form mastered by Obi-Wan Kenobi.
4. **Ataru** — acrobatic and aggressive, used by Yoda.
5. **Shien / Djem So** — counterattacking form used by Anakin Skywalker.
6. **Niman** — a balanced, diplomatic form.
7. **Juyo / Vaapad** — the most aggressive form, channeled by Mace Windu.

## Notable lightsabers

- **Anakin Skywalker's lightsaber** — later wielded by Luke and then Rey.
- **The Darksaber** — a unique black-bladed saber tied to Mandalorian leadership.
- **Darth Maul's double-bladed saber** — a saberstaff with blades on both ends.
- **Kylo Ren's crossguard saber** — an unstable cracked kyber crystal vents through side emitters.
