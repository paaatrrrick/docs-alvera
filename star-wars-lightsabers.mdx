# Lightsabers

The lightsaber is the signature weapon of both the Jedi Order and the Sith. Each blade is powered by a kyber crystal, which determines the color and behavior of the plasma blade.

## Construction

A lightsaber is built around a focusing crystal housed inside a metal hilt. Jedi traditionally construct their own sabers as a rite of passage, attuning the crystal to themselves through the Force.

## Blade colors

- **Blue** carried by Jedi Guardians who focus on combat
- **Green** wielded by Jedi Consulars and those attuned to the living Force
- **Red** produced by Sith who bleed their crystals through the dark side
