## Overview

The music of Star Wars is among the most recognizable in film history. Composer John Williams scored every entry in the Skywalker saga, weaving recurring leitmotifs that follow characters, factions, and ideas across decades of storytelling.

## John Williams

John Williams composed the original score for *A New Hope* in 1977 and returned for every numbered Skywalker saga film through *The Rise of Skywalker*. His sweeping orchestral approach, performed by the London Symphony Orchestra, defined the sound of the franchise and reshaped the modern film score.

## Iconic themes

A handful of themes recur across the saga and are instantly tied to specific characters or ideas.

- **Main Title** — the opening fanfare that plays under every Skywalker saga crawl.
- **The Force Theme (Binary Sunset)** — introduced in *A New Hope* and tied to the Jedi, hope, and Luke Skywalker.
- **The Imperial March (Darth Vader's Theme)** — debuted in *The Empire Strikes Back* and signals the Empire and Vader.
- **Duel of the Fates** — choral piece from *The Phantom Menace* that scores the lightsaber duel with Darth Maul.
- **Princess Leia's Theme** — a romantic, lyrical motif introduced in *A New Hope*.
- **Across the Stars** — the love theme for Anakin and Padmé from *Attack of the Clones*.
- **Rey's Theme** — Williams's central motif for the sequel trilogy.
- **Cantina Band (Mad About Me)** — the diegetic jizz number performed at Chalmun's Cantina on Tatooine.

## Other composers

While Williams scored the saga films, other composers have shaped the wider franchise.

- **Michael Giacchino** — *Rogue One: A Star Wars Story*.
- **John Powell** — *Solo: A Star Wars Story*, with a new theme by Williams.
- **Ludwig Göransson** — *The Mandalorian* and *The Book of Boba Fett*.
- **Natalie Holt** — *Obi-Wan Kenobi*.
- **Nicholas Britell** and **Brandon Roberts** — *Andor*.
- **Kevin Kiner** — *The Clone Wars*, *Rebels*, and *Ahsoka*.

## In-universe music

Music also exists inside the galaxy itself. The Modal Nodes perform "Cantina Band" at the Mos Eisley Cantina, the Max Rebo Band plays in Jabba's palace, and the Mandalorian culture features distinctive horn calls and percussion in *The Mandalorian*.
