# Kyber Crystals

Kyber crystals are rare, Force-attuned crystals that power lightsabers and, on a far larger scale, Imperial superweapons.

## Attunement

A kyber crystal resonates with the Force user who claims it. The bond influences the color and focus of the resulting blade.

## Strategic value

Because large kyber crystals can amplify destructive energy, control of kyber deposits became a strategic priority for the Empire.
