# Sith Lords

The Sith are an ancient order of Force users devoted to the dark side. Driven by passion and ambition, they seek power above all else.

## The Rule of Two

For much of their history the Sith followed the Rule of Two: one master to embody power, and one apprentice to crave it. This kept the order hidden while ensuring each generation grew stronger.

## Notable Sith

Across the ages, Sith Lords have toppled republics and corrupted Jedi. Their influence echoes through the [Force](/star-wars-force) itself.
