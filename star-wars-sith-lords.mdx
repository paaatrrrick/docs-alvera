## Overview

The Sith are an ancient order of Force-wielders devoted to the dark side. Bound by the Rule of Two—one master, one apprentice—they pursue power, knowledge, and domination across millennia of galactic history.

## The Rule of Two

Established by Darth Bane, the Rule of Two ensures the Sith remain hidden and potent:

- **The Master** embodies the power of the dark side.
- **The Apprentice** craves it.

Only two Sith may exist at any time, preventing the infighting that destroyed earlier Sith orders.

## Signature abilities

### Force lightning

A torrent of dark-side energy channeled through the hands, favored by Sidious and Dooku.

### Force choke

Telekinetic strangulation used to intimidate and execute, infamously wielded by Vader.

### Sith alchemy

Ancient practices that warp creatures, weapons, and even holocrons to serve dark purposes.

## Sith artifacts

- **Holocrons**: Pyramidal repositories of forbidden Sith knowledge.
- **Kyber crystals**: Bled red through dark-side corruption to power Sith lightsabers.
- **Sith temples**: Hidden on worlds like Moraband, Exegol, and Malachor.

## Legacy

Though the Sith were believed destroyed at the Battle of Endor, Darth Sidious endured in secret on Exegol, proving the dark side's grip on the galaxy is rarely broken cleanly. Their influence shapes every era of the Skywalker saga.
