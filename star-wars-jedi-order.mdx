## Overview

The Jedi Order is an ancient monastic, academic, meritocratic, and quasi-religious organization devoted to the study and use of the light side of the Force. For over a thousand generations, the Jedi served as guardians of peace and justice in the Galactic Republic.

## The Jedi Code

The Jedi follow a code that guides their conduct and connection to the Force:

> There is no emotion, there is peace.
> There is no ignorance, there is knowledge.
> There is no passion, there is serenity.
> There is no chaos, there is harmony.
> There is no death, there is the Force.

## Ranks

| Rank | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Youngling | A Force-sensitive child training in the basics at the Jedi Temple. |
| Padawan | An apprentice paired with a Jedi Knight or Master for individual training. |
| Jedi Knight | A full member of the Order who has passed the Trials. |
| Jedi Master | A Knight who has trained a Padawan to Knighthood or shown exceptional wisdom. |
| Grand Master | The leader of the Jedi Order, traditionally the wisest member of the Council. |

## The Jedi High Council

The Jedi High Council was a body of twelve Masters that governed the Order from the Jedi Temple on Coruscant. Members debated galactic matters, assigned missions, and oversaw the training of new Jedi.

## Notable Jedi

- **Yoda** — Grand Master who trained Jedi for over 800 years.
- **Mace Windu** — Senior Council member and master of Vaapad.
- **Qui-Gon Jinn** — Maverick Master who discovered Anakin Skywalker.
- **Obi-Wan Kenobi** — Master of Anakin and later Luke Skywalker.
- **Anakin Skywalker** — The Chosen One, prophesied to bring balance to the Force.
- **Ahsoka Tano** — Anakin's Padawan who left the Order before its fall.
- **Luke Skywalker** — Rebuilder of the Jedi Order after the Empire's collapse.

## Fall and legacy

The Jedi Order was nearly destroyed during Order 66, when clone troopers turned on their Jedi commanders at the end of the Clone Wars. A handful of survivors, including Yoda and Obi-Wan, went into hiding. The Order's traditions were carried forward by Luke Skywalker and later by Rey, ensuring the light side of the Force endured.
