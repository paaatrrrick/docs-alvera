## Overview

Lightsaber combat in the Star Wars galaxy is organized into seven traditional forms. Each form represents a distinct philosophy and style, developed over millennia by Jedi and Sith duelists to address the evolving threats they faced.

## The seven forms

### Form I: Shii-Cho

The oldest form, Shii-Cho ("The Way of the Sarlacc") is the foundation taught to every Jedi youngling. It emphasizes broad, sweeping strikes and is most effective against multiple opponents or non-lightsaber weapons.

### Form II: Makashi

Makashi ("The Way of the Ysalamiri") is a refined dueling form built for lightsaber-to-lightsaber combat. It favors precision, footwork, and economy of motion. Count Dooku is its most famous practitioner.

### Form III: Soresu

Soresu ("The Way of the Mynock") is a purely defensive form designed to deflect blaster fire and outlast aggressive opponents. Obi-Wan Kenobi mastered Soresu after the death of Qui-Gon Jinn.

### Form IV: Ataru

Ataru ("The Way of the Hawk-Bat") is an acrobatic, high-energy form that leverages Force-assisted leaps and spins. Yoda relied on Ataru in his most demanding duels.

### Form V: Shien / Djem So

Form V splits into two variants. Shien deflects blaster bolts back at attackers, while Djem So channels incoming saber strikes into powerful counterattacks. Anakin Skywalker favored Djem So.

### Form VI: Niman

Niman ("The Way of the Rancor") is a balanced, diplomatic form that blends elements of the earlier styles. It is well suited to Jedi who divide their training between combat and Force techniques.

### Form VII: Juyo / Vaapad

The most aggressive form, Juyo ("The Ferocity Form") channels controlled emotion into unpredictable attacks. Mace Windu developed Vaapad, a refined variant that walks the edge of the dark side without falling into it.

## Choosing a form

| Form | Strength | Best against |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Shii-Cho | Versatility | Multiple opponents |
| Makashi | Precision | Lightsaber duelists |
| Soresu | Defense | Blaster-wielding enemies |
| Ataru | Mobility | Single, slower opponents |
| Shien / Djem So | Counterattack | Aggressive duelists |
| Niman | Balance | General-purpose combat |
| Juyo / Vaapad | Ferocity | Dark side adversaries |
