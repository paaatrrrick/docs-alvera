## Overview

Weapons in the Star Wars galaxy range from elegant lightsabers wielded by Force users to mass-produced blasters carried by soldiers and bounty hunters. The most powerful weapons of all are the planet-killing superweapons built by the Empire and First Order.

## Lightsabers

Lightsabers are the signature weapon of the Jedi and Sith. Each saber is powered by a kyber crystal that determines its blade color and characteristics.

- **Blue blades**: Traditionally carried by Jedi Guardians who focus on combat, such as Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi.
- **Green blades**: Associated with Jedi Consulars who emphasize diplomacy and the Force, including Yoda and Luke Skywalker.
- **Purple blade**: Famously wielded by Mace Windu, reflecting his unique balance between light and dark.
- **Red blades**: Created by Sith and dark side users through the corruption of kyber crystals.
- **Darksaber**: A unique black-bladed lightsaber forged by the first Mandalorian Jedi, Tarre Vizsla.

## Blasters

Blasters are the most common ranged weapons across the galaxy, firing bolts of energized plasma.

### Blaster pistols

- **DL-44 heavy blaster pistol**: Han Solo's modified sidearm, prized for its stopping power.
- **EC-17 hold-out blaster**: A compact pistol favored by scout troopers.

### Blaster rifles

- **E-11 blaster rifle**: The standard-issue weapon of Imperial stormtroopers.
- **DC-15A blaster rifle**: Carried by clone troopers during the Clone Wars.
- **A280 blaster rifle**: A powerful rifle used by Rebel Alliance soldiers.

## Specialty weapons

- **Bowcaster**: A traditional Wookiee crossbow that fires energized quarrels, most famously used by Chewbacca.
- **Electrostaff**: A melee weapon resistant to lightsabers, carried by MagnaGuards and Imperial Royal Guards.
- **Amban phase-pulse blaster**: The long rifle used by the Mandalorian Din Djarin.
- **Thermal detonator**: A small but devastating explosive carried by bounty hunters and soldiers alike.

## Superweapons

The galaxy's most feared weapons are capable of destroying entire planets or fleets.

- **Death Star**: The Empire's moon-sized battle station, armed with a superlaser that destroyed Alderaan.
- **Death Star II**: A larger, more powerful version built over the forest moon of Endor.
- **Starkiller Base**: A First Order superweapon built into a planet, capable of destroying multiple systems at once.
- **Sun Crusher**: A small but immensely powerful prototype designed to trigger supernovas in stars.
