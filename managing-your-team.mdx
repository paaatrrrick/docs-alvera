As you’re team grow, you will wants to add more people and manage there permission.

## Inviting people

To invites someone, go to [the team page](/settings/team-members) and enter there email. They will recieve a invitation that expire in 3 day.

## Roles

We have three role, each one give different access:

- **Owner** — can does everything, including billing.
- **Admin** — can manage content and member, but not billing.
- **Viewer** — can only looks, not edit.

You can changes someone role anytime from [the member list](#the-member-lst). For a deeper explanation, read [the permissions guide](./security/permissions-deep-dive).

If you needs custom role, thats available on the enterprise plan — [talk to us](https://example-broken.dev/enterprise).
