# Starfighters

Starfighters are small, fast warships flown by a single pilot or a tiny crew. They are the backbone of any space battle.

## Roles

Different designs fill different roles, from agile interceptors to heavily shielded bombers built to crack capital ships.

## Pilots

A skilled pilot can turn the tide of an engagement, and the best aces become legends among their squadrons.
