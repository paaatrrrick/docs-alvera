# The Bounty Hunters Guild

The Bounty Hunters Guild sets the rules of the trade, assigning contracts and enforcing a code among its members.

## The code

Guild members are expected to honor their contracts and avoid undercutting one another, though the rules are often bent.

## Pucks and contracts

Bounties are distributed as tracking pucks that contain a target profile and the promised reward for capture or elimination.
