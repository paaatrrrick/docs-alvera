# Smugglers

Smugglers operate in the gray spaces of galactic law, moving goods past blockades and customs for the right price.

## The trade

A good smuggler needs a fast ship, a cool head, and a willingness to take on jobs that legitimate haulers would refuse.

## Reputation

The best smugglers build networks of contacts across the Outer Rim, balancing debts to crime lords against the constant risk of capture.
