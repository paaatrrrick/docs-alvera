# The Rebel Alliance

The Alliance to Restore the Republic was a coalition of worlds, soldiers, and idealists united against Imperial tyranny.

## Origins

The Alliance grew from scattered cells of resistance into an organized military force capable of challenging the Empire directly.

## Hit-and-run tactics

Outnumbered and outgunned, the Rebels relied on mobility, surprise, and the loyalty of their pilots to survive.
