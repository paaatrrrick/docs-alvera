# Podracing

Podracing is a high-speed sport in which competitors pilot small pods pulled by massive engines. The races are wildly dangerous and popular across the Outer Rim.

## The pods

A podracer consists of a cockpit tethered to two enormous turbine engines by energy binders. Pilots need lightning reflexes to survive a single lap.

## Tatooine circuit

The Boonta Eve Classic on Tatooine is among the most famous races, drawing crowds and gamblers from across the sector.
