## Overview

The Star Wars galaxy is filled with a wide range of vehicles used for transport, exploration, and warfare. While starships handle interstellar travel, ground and atmospheric vehicles play a crucial role in planetary operations.

## Ground vehicles

### Speeders

Speeders are repulsorlift vehicles that hover above the ground, used for both civilian transport and military operations.

- **Landspeeder**: Luke Skywalker's X-34 landspeeder on Tatooine is one of the most recognizable civilian models.
- **Speeder bike**: Fast, single-rider vehicles famously used by scout troopers on Endor.

### Walkers

Walkers are bipedal or quadrupedal armored vehicles used primarily by the Galactic Empire and the First Order.

| Walker | Type | Notable appearance |
| --- | --- | --- |
| AT-AT | Quadrupedal heavy assault | Battle of Hoth |
| AT-ST | Bipedal scout walker | Battle of Endor |
| AT-M6 | Quadrupedal heavy walker | Battle of Crait |

## Atmospheric craft

### Airspeeders

Airspeeders operate within planetary atmospheres and are often used for patrol or combat support.

- **T-47 airspeeder (snowspeeder)**: Used by Rebel pilots during the Battle of Hoth.
- **Naboo N-1 starfighter**: A sleek dual-purpose craft capable of both atmospheric and space flight.

### Pod racers

Pod racers are high-speed racing vehicles featured prominently on Tatooine, most notably in the Boonta Eve Classic won by a young Anakin Skywalker.

## Transports

Larger transports move troops, cargo, and refugees across the galaxy.

- **Sandcrawler**: Massive Jawa transports that scavenge the deserts of Tatooine.
- **Imperial troop transport**: Used to move stormtroopers across hostile terrain.
- **A-A5 speeder truck**: A common civilian cargo hauler seen on many worlds.

## Cultural significance

Vehicles in Star Wars are more than transportation — they reflect the technology, culture, and conflicts of the era. From the rugged landspeeders of frontier worlds to the towering walkers of the Empire, each vehicle helps build the lived-in feel of a galaxy far, far away.
