## New features

- **Personal website launch.** The site is live with a home page, a [work history](/work-experience) timeline, a [projects](/projects) showcase, and dedicated pages for reading list, talks, testimonials, and current focus ("now").
- **Colophon page.** A new page documenting the tools, fonts, and stack behind the site.
- **Blog.** Long-form posts are now browsable from the site, with permalinks for individual articles.
- **Contact page.** A direct way to get in touch, including social links.
- **Custom 404.** Missing pages now land on a branded not-found screen instead of a default error.
