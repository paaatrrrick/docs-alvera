## Overview

The Force is the metaphysical energy field that binds the Star Wars galaxy together. It flows through all living things and can be sensed, channeled, and shaped by Force-sensitive individuals.

## The two sides

The Force has two opposing aspects, each tied to the emotional state and intent of its user.

### Light side

The light side is associated with calm, compassion, and selflessness. It is studied by the Jedi, who emphasize discipline and emotional balance.

- Practiced by: **Jedi Order**
- Core values: peace, knowledge, defense
- Typical lightsaber colors: blue, green, yellow, purple

### Dark side

The dark side is fueled by passion, anger, and fear. It is faster to access but corrupts its users over time.

- Practiced by: **Sith Order**, Inquisitors, Knights of Ren
- Core values: power, ambition, dominance
- Typical lightsaber color: red (created through "bleeding" a kyber crystal)

## Common Force abilities

| Ability | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Telekinesis | Lifting and moving objects without physical contact |
| Mind trick | Influencing the thoughts of weak-minded beings |
| Force jump | Augmenting physical leaps and reflexes |
| Force lightning | Channeling raw dark-side energy as electricity |
| Force healing | Transferring life energy to mend injuries |
| Foresight | Glimpsing possible futures or distant events |

## Force traditions

While the Jedi and Sith are the most prominent, the galaxy is home to many Force-using groups.

- **Jedi Order** — Guardians of peace and justice in the Old Republic.
- **Sith Order** — Ancient enemies of the Jedi, ruled by the Rule of Two.
- **Nightsisters of Dathomir** — Witches who practice Force-based magick.
- **Guardians of the Whills** — Spiritual protectors of the kyber temple on Jedha.
- **The Bendu** — A neutral Force entity embodying balance.

## Balance of the Force

A central theme of the Skywalker saga is the prophecy of the Chosen One who will bring balance to the Force. This balance is interpreted as the destruction of the Sith and the restoration of harmony between life and the Force itself.
