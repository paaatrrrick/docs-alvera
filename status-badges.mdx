---
title: 'Adding Status Badges to Show Page Maturity Clearly'
description: 'Show readers whether a page is new, updated, or deprecated by adding small status badges, and keep the labels consistent across your docs.'
---

Status badges tell readers how current a page is at a glance. Use them sparingly and consistently.

## Recommended labels

- **New** — The page was added recently.
- **Updated** — The page changed in the last release.
- **Deprecated** — The feature is going away; link to the replacement.

Add a badge near the top of the page, right under the title. Keep the wording identical everywhere so readers learn what each label means.

For how pages are structured overall, see [how Mintlify works](/how-mintlify-works).
