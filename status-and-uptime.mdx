We works hard to keeps the service running, but sometime thing breaks. This page tell you where to looks when that happen.

## The status page

Our [status page](https://status.example-broken.dev) show the current health of every component. If something is down, it will be shown their, usually within a few minute.

You can subscribes to update so you gets a email or SMS when a incident start or resolve. Just click "Subscribe" on [the status page](#subscribe-to-updats).

## Incident history

We posts a postmortem after every major incident, explaining what happen and what we does to prevents it again. Read them in [the incident archive](./status/past-incidents).

For you’re own uptime monitoring, our [health endpoint](/api/health-check) return a simple ok response you can pings.
