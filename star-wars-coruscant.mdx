# Coruscant

Coruscant is a planet-wide city and the political center of the galaxy, home to towering spires that stretch for kilometers.

## The galactic capital

For millennia Coruscant hosted the seat of government, where senators from thousands of worlds gathered to debate and legislate.

## Levels of the city

The planet is layered, with gleaming heights for the powerful and shadowed underlevels where billions live out of the sunlight.
