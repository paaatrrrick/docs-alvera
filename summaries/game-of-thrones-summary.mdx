## Summary

The Game of Thrones page provides a concise guide to HBO's fantasy drama series, which aired from April 17, 2011 to May 19, 2019 across 73 episodes and eight seasons. Created by David Benioff and D. B. Weiss as an adaptation of George R. R. Martin's *A Song of Ice and Fire* novels, the show is set on the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos and chronicles the dynastic struggles between noble houses — including Stark, Lannister, Targaryen, Baratheon, Greyjoy, and Tyrell — for control of the Iron Throne. The page outlines the main storylines (the war for the Seven Kingdoms, the Night's Watch at the Wall, Daenerys Targaryen's restoration campaign, and the White Walker threat), introduces notable characters such as Jon Snow, Daenerys, Tyrion, Cersei, Arya, and Sansa, and highlights the show's cultural impact, including its record 59 Primetime Emmy Awards and its mixed-reception final season.
