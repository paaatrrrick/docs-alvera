## Summary

The "Creating an incident" Notion playbook outlines the steps for opening an incident when a customer-impacting issue arises. Engineers kick things off in Slack with `/inc` or `/incident` to launch the Incident.io workflow, then name the incident, set its severity per the team's severity guide, and fill in optional fields such as on-call rotation, affected product area, and detection method when known. Incident.io automatically spins up a dedicated Slack channel and assigns the on-call engineer to lead the response, after which the reporter joins the channel, pastes in the originating Slack thread for context, and continues all communication there alongside the auto-added @success Slack group. The CSM owns customer-facing communication, while engineers are only looped into the customer Slack channel temporarily for highly technical discussions and leave once the issue is resolved.
