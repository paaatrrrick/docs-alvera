## Summary

The Star Wars page offers a concise guide to George Lucas's epic space-opera franchise, which began with the 1977 film *A New Hope* and has expanded into films, television series, novels, comics, and games set in a galaxy far, far away. It outlines the nine-film Skywalker saga, grouped into the original trilogy (1977-1983), the prequel trilogy (1999-2005), and the sequel trilogy (2015-2019), and explains core concepts including the Force as a binding metaphysical energy, the opposing Jedi and Sith orders representing the light and dark sides, and the iconic lightsaber weapons whose blade colors signal allegiance. The page introduces notable characters such as Luke Skywalker, Darth Vader, Princess Leia, Han Solo, Yoda, and Rey alongside their affiliations and roles, and notes that the franchise extends well beyond the main saga through standalone films like *Rogue One* and *Solo* and acclaimed series including *The Mandalorian*, *Andor*, and *Ahsoka*.
