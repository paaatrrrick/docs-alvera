## Summary

The Star Wars page offers a concise guide to George Lucas's epic space-opera franchise, which began with the 1977 film *A New Hope* and has expanded into films, television, novels, comics, and games set in a galaxy far, far away. The page outlines the nine-film Skywalker saga split across three trilogies — the original (1977-1983), prequel (1999-2005), and sequel (2015-2019) trilogies — and explains key concepts including the Force as a metaphysical energy field, the opposing Jedi and Sith orders representing the light and dark sides, and the iconic lightsabers whose blade colors signal allegiance. It introduces notable characters such as Luke Skywalker, Darth Vader, Princess Leia, Han Solo, Yoda, and Rey, and notes how the franchise extends beyond the main saga through standalone films like *Rogue One* and *Solo* and acclaimed series including *The Mandalorian*, *Andor*, and *Ahsoka*.
