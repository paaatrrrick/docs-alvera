# The Clone Wars

The Clone Wars were a pan-galactic conflict fought between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems. The war reshaped the galaxy and set the stage for the rise of the Empire.

## Origins

Tensions had been building for years as thousands of star systems threatened to secede from the Republic. The discovery of a secret clone army on Kamino gave the Republic the means to wage war.

## Key campaigns

The fighting spanned countless worlds, from the factories of Geonosis to the sinkholes of Utapau. Both sides relied heavily on droid and clone armies.
