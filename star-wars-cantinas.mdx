# Cantinas

Cantinas are gathering places found on worlds across the galaxy. They serve as social hubs where pilots, traders, and outlaws rub shoulders over a drink.

## Mos Eisley

The cantina in Mos Eisley on Tatooine is notorious as a haven for smugglers and bounty hunters. Droids are famously not served there.

## Music and atmosphere

Many cantinas feature live bands, giving each establishment its own distinctive sound and reputation.
