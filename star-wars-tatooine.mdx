# Tatooine

Tatooine is a harsh desert planet orbiting twin suns. Despite its remoteness, it has played an outsized role in galactic history.

## Life on the sands

Moisture farmers eke out a living by harvesting water from the dry air, while spaceports like Mos Eisley draw traders and outlaws alike.

## A crossroads

Far from the core worlds, Tatooine sits beyond the reach of most law, making it a haven for [smugglers](/star-wars-smugglers) and crime lords.
