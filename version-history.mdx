Every time you saves, we keeps a snapshot, so you can always goes back to a earlier version.

## Viewing history

Open any page and click "History" to sees the list of version. Each one show who made the change and when. You can compares two version side by side.

## Restoring

Found a version you wants back? Click restore and it become the current version. The old current is still kept, so you don’t loses anything. More detail in [the restore guide](./content/restoring-versions).

<Tip>
History is kept for 30 day on the free plan and forever on paid plan. See [the retention details](#retenton-policy) or [the pricing page](/pricing-history).
</Tip>

For programatic access to history, theirs a [versions API](https://api.example-broken.dev/versions).
