## Overview

Star Wars is an epic space-opera franchise created by George Lucas. It began with the 1977 film *Star Wars* (later subtitled *A New Hope*) and has since grown to include films, television series, novels, comics, and games set in a galaxy far, far away.

## The Skywalker saga

The core saga spans nine films divided into three trilogies:

- **Original trilogy (1977-1983)**: *A New Hope*, *The Empire Strikes Back*, *Return of the Jedi*
- **Prequel trilogy (1999-2005)**: *The Phantom Menace*, *Attack of the Clones*, *Revenge of the Sith*
- **Sequel trilogy (2015-2019)**: *The Force Awakens*, *The Last Jedi*, *The Rise of Skywalker*

## Key concepts

### The Force

The Force is a metaphysical energy field that binds the galaxy together. Force-sensitive individuals can harness it for telekinesis, foresight, and combat.

### The Jedi and the Sith

- **Jedi**: Guardians of peace who follow the light side of the Force.
- **Sith**: Practitioners of the dark side who seek power and domination.

### Lightsabers

Lightsabers are the iconic energy weapons wielded by Jedi and Sith. Blade colors typically reflect their user's allegiance, with blue and green for Jedi and red for Sith.

## Iconic factions

- **Galactic Republic**: The democratic government of the prequel era.
- **Galactic Empire**: The autocratic regime that replaced the Republic.
- **Rebel Alliance**: Resistance movement that opposed the Empire.
- **First Order**: Successor to the Empire in the sequel trilogy.
- **Resistance**: Rebel-aligned group opposing the First Order.

## Beyond the films

The franchise extends well past the main saga, including standalone films like *Rogue One* and *Solo*, and acclaimed series such as *The Mandalorian*, *Andor*, and *Ahsoka*.

### Notable series

- **The Mandalorian**: Follows a lone bounty hunter and the foundling Grogu.
- **Andor**: A grounded spy thriller set before *Rogue One*.
- **Ahsoka**: Continues the story of former Jedi Ahsoka Tano.
- **The Clone Wars**: Animated series bridging *Episode II* and *Episode III*.
- **Rebels**: Animated series set between the prequel and original trilogies.

## Iconic ships and vehicles

- **Millennium Falcon**: Han Solo's modified YT-1300 light freighter.
- **X-wing starfighter**: The Rebel Alliance's signature starfighter.
- **TIE fighter**: The Empire's standard short-range fighter.
- **Star Destroyer**: Massive Imperial warships that project galactic power.
- **Death Star**: Planet-destroying Imperial battle station.
