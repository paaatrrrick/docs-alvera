## Overview

Star Wars is a space opera franchise created by George Lucas. The original film premiered in 1977 and has since grown into a sprawling universe of films, television series, novels, comics, and games set "a long time ago in a galaxy far, far away."

## Eras of the saga

The Skywalker Saga spans three trilogies, each set in a distinct era of galactic history.

## The Force

The Force is an energy field that binds the galaxy together. Practitioners follow one of two paths:

- **The light side** — used by the Jedi, emphasizing peace, knowledge, and selflessness.
- **The dark side** — used by the Sith, fueled by passion, anger, and the pursuit of power.

## Beyond the films

The franchise extends well past the main films:

- **The Mandalorian** — a bounty hunter's adventures in the post-Imperial galaxy.
- **Andor** — the origins of the Rebel Alliance.
- **The Clone Wars** — animated series set between Episodes II and III.
- **Rogue One** — the mission to steal the Death Star plans.
