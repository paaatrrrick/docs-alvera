## Overview

The Sith Order was an ancient sect of Force-sensitive warriors devoted to the dark side. Born from fallen Jedi who embraced passion and power, the Sith waged war against the Jedi Order for thousands of years, culminating in the rise of the Galactic Empire under Darth Sidious.

## The Sith Code

Where the Jedi sought serenity, the Sith embraced raw emotion. Their code is a manifesto of personal ambition:

- Peace is a lie, there is only passion.
- Through passion, I gain strength.
- Through strength, I gain power.
- Through power, I gain victory.
- Through victory, my chains are broken.
- The Force shall free me.

## The Rule of Two

After the Sith were nearly destroyed at the Battle of Ruusan, Darth Bane instituted the Rule of Two: only a master and an apprentice may exist at any time. The master embodies power; the apprentice covets it. This rule ensured the Sith survived in shadow for a millennium before Sidious finally toppled the Republic.

## Ranks

- **Dark Lord of the Sith** — the master, the highest authority.
- **Sith Apprentice** — the student, sworn to overthrow the master when ready.
- **Sith Acolyte** — informal aspirants and inquisitors who serve the Order but stand outside the Rule of Two.

## Sith weapons and artifacts

- **Red lightsabers** — crystals are bled through focused rage, turning them crimson.
- **Sith holocrons** — pyramidal repositories of forbidden knowledge.
- **Sith alchemy** — the manipulation of flesh, stone, and metal through the dark side.

## Legacy

Though the Sith Order ended with Darth Sidious's final death above Exegol, their teachings endured through cults, acolytes, and the Knights of Ren — a reminder that the dark side, like the light, is never truly extinguished.
