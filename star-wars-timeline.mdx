## Overview

Events in Star Wars are dated relative to the Battle of Yavin, the climactic battle at the end of *A New Hope*. Years before that battle are labeled **BBY** (Before the Battle of Yavin), and years after are labeled **ABY** (After the Battle of Yavin).

## Key dates

| Year | Event |
| --- | --- |
| ~25,000 BBY | Founding of the Galactic Republic and the Jedi Order |
| 32 BBY | Invasion of Naboo (*The Phantom Menace*) |
| 22 BBY | Outbreak of the Clone Wars (*Attack of the Clones*) |
| 19 BBY | Order 66 and the rise of the Galactic Empire (*Revenge of the Sith*) |
| 5 BBY | Formation of the Rebel Alliance (*Andor*, *Rebels*) |
| 0 BBY | Battle of Yavin and destruction of the first Death Star (*A New Hope*) |
| 3 ABY | Battle of Hoth (*The Empire Strikes Back*) |
| 4 ABY | Battle of Endor and fall of the Empire (*Return of the Jedi*) |
| 9 ABY | Era of *The Mandalorian* and *Ahsoka* |
| 34 ABY | Starkiller Base attack (*The Force Awakens*) |
| 35 ABY | Battle of Exegol (*The Rise of Skywalker*) |

## Eras at a glance

- **High Republic** (~500-100 BBY): The Jedi Order at its peak, exploring the frontier.
- **Fall of the Jedi** (32-19 BBY): The prequel trilogy and Clone Wars.
- **Reign of the Empire** (19-5 BBY): Imperial expansion and growing dissent.
- **Age of Rebellion** (5 BBY-4 ABY): The Galactic Civil War.
- **New Republic** (4-28 ABY): Reconstruction and the rise of the First Order.
- **Rise of the First Order** (28-35 ABY): The sequel trilogy conflict.
