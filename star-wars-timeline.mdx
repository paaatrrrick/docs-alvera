## Overview

The Star Wars galaxy spans thousands of years of recorded history. Dates are measured against the Battle of Yavin, with **BBY** ("Before the Battle of Yavin") and **ABY** ("After the Battle of Yavin") anchoring every major event.

## Era summary

| Era | Approximate years | Defining conflict |
| --- | --- | --- |
| High Republic | 500-100 BBY | Jedi expansion and the Nihil crisis |
| Fall of the Jedi | 32-19 BBY | Clone Wars and Order 66 |
| Reign of the Empire | 19-5 BBY | Imperial consolidation |
| Age of Rebellion | 5 BBY-4 ABY | Galactic Civil War |
| New Republic | 5-28 ABY | Rebuilding and the rise of the First Order |
| Rise of the First Order | 28-35 ABY | Resistance vs. First Order and Final Order |

## Key events

### The Clone Wars (22-19 BBY)

A galaxy-spanning conflict between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems. Ends with Order 66, the near-extinction of the Jedi, and the founding of the Galactic Empire.

### The Galactic Civil War (2 BBY-4 ABY)

Fought between the Rebel Alliance and the Empire. The Rebellion's victories at Yavin and Endor topple the Emperor and shatter Imperial control.

### The First Order conflict (34-35 ABY)

The Resistance, led by General Leia Organa, opposes the First Order's resurgence. The conflict ends on Exegol with the defeat of the resurrected Emperor Palpatine.
