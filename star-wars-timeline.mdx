## Overview

The Star Wars saga spans thousands of years of in-universe history. This page summarizes the key events in canonical chronological order, measured in BBY (Before the Battle of Yavin) and ABY (After the Battle of Yavin).

## Eras at a glance

| Era | Approximate dates | Defining events |
| --- | --- | --- |
| High Republic | 500-100 BBY | Golden age of the Jedi Order |
| Fall of the Republic | 32-19 BBY | Trade Federation invasion through Clone Wars |
| Reign of the Empire | 19-5 BBY | Jedi Purge and Imperial expansion |
| Age of Rebellion | 5 BBY-4 ABY | Formation of the Alliance to the fall of the Empire |
| New Republic | 5-28 ABY | Rebuilding the galaxy after Endor |
| Rise of the First Order | 28-35 ABY | Sequel trilogy and the final defeat of the Sith |

## Key events

### Fall of the Republic (32-19 BBY)

- **32 BBY** — The Trade Federation blockades Naboo. Qui-Gon Jinn discovers Anakin Skywalker on Tatooine.
- **22 BBY** — The First Battle of Geonosis ignites the Clone Wars between the Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems.
- **19 BBY** — Order 66 is executed. Anakin becomes Darth Vader, the Republic becomes the Galactic Empire, and the Jedi Order is nearly destroyed.

### Reign of the Empire (19-5 BBY)

- **18 BBY** — Surviving Jedi go into hiding. Obi-Wan Kenobi watches over Luke on Tatooine; Yoda exiles himself to Dagobah.
- **5 BBY** — Construction begins on the first Death Star. Rebel cells coalesce into the Alliance to Restore the Republic.

### Age of Rebellion (5 BBY-4 ABY)

- **0 BBY** — Rebels steal the Death Star plans (*Rogue One*). Luke Skywalker destroys the Death Star at the Battle of Yavin.
- **3 ABY** — The Empire strikes back at Hoth. Luke begins Jedi training with Yoda and learns Vader is his father.
- **4 ABY** — The Battle of Endor. Vader kills Emperor Palpatine and dies redeemed. The second Death Star is destroyed.

### New Republic (5-28 ABY)

- **5 ABY** — The Battle of Jakku ends the Galactic Civil War. The Empire signs the Galactic Concordance.
- **9 ABY** — Din Djarin protects Grogu (*The Mandalorian*) as Imperial remnants regroup.
- **28 ABY** — Ben Solo turns to the dark side as Kylo Ren and destroys Luke's new Jedi temple.

### Rise of the First Order (34-35 ABY)

- **34 ABY** — The First Order destroys the Hosnian system. Rey finds Luke on Ahch-To.
- **35 ABY** — Rey defeats the resurrected Emperor Palpatine on Exegol, ending the Sith.
