## Overview

The Star Wars saga spans tens of thousands of years of in-universe history. Dates are measured relative to the Battle of Yavin (BBY = before, ABY = after), the climactic event of *A New Hope*.

## Major eras

| Era | Approximate dates | Highlights |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Dawn of the Jedi | ~25,000 BBY | Founding of the Jedi Order on Tython. |
| Old Republic | 25,000-1,000 BBY | Long peace punctuated by repeated wars with the Sith. |
| High Republic | 500-100 BBY | Golden age of the Jedi explored in recent novels and comics. |
| Fall of the Republic | 32-19 BBY | Trade Federation blockade, Clone Wars, and Order 66. |
| Reign of the Empire | 19-0 BBY | Galactic Empire rules; Rebel Alliance forms. |
| Galactic Civil War | 0 BBY-4 ABY | Battles of Yavin, Hoth, and Endor. |
| New Republic | 4-28 ABY | Empire collapses; First Order emerges in secret. |
| Rise of the First Order | 28-35 ABY | Sequel trilogy conflict ending in the defeat of the Final Order. |

## Pivotal events

- **Ruusan Reformations (1,000 BBY)** — The Sith are thought destroyed; the Republic disarms.
- **Invasion of Naboo (32 BBY)** — Anakin Skywalker is discovered; Palpatine rises to Chancellor.
- **Clone Wars (22-19 BBY)** — Galaxy-spanning conflict between the Republic and the Separatists.
- **Order 66 (19 BBY)** — Clone troopers execute their Jedi commanders, near-destroying the Order.
- **Battle of Yavin (0 BBY/ABY)** — Luke Skywalker destroys the first Death Star.
- **Battle of Endor (4 ABY)** — Emperor Palpatine dies; the Empire fractures.
- **Battle of Exegol (35 ABY)** — The Final Order is defeated and the Skywalker saga closes.

## Where to start

If you're new to the timeline, the films remain the best entry point. From there, *The Clone Wars* and *Rebels* fill in the prequel-to-original gap, while *The Mandalorian* and *Ahsoka* explore the years between *Return of the Jedi* and the sequel trilogy.
