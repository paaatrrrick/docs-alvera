## Overview

The Star Wars galaxy spans tens of thousands of years of in-universe history. Events are dated relative to the Battle of Yavin (BBY — Before the Battle of Yavin; ABY — After the Battle of Yavin), when the first Death Star was destroyed in *A New Hope*.

## Major eras

| Era | Approximate range | Key events |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Old Republic | 25,000 - 1,000 BBY | Founding of the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic |
| Fall of the Jedi | 32 - 19 BBY | Prequel trilogy, Clone Wars, Order 66 |
| Reign of the Empire | 19 - 5 BBY | Imperial rule, early Rebellion, *Andor*, *Rebels* |
| Age of Rebellion | 5 BBY - 4 ABY | Original trilogy, fall of the Emperor |
| New Republic | 5 - 28 ABY | *The Mandalorian*, *Ahsoka*, rebuilding the galaxy |
| Rise of the First Order | 28 - 35 ABY | Sequel trilogy, fall of the First Order |

## Key turning points

### The Clone Wars (22 - 19 BBY)

A galaxy-spanning conflict between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems. The war was orchestrated by Darth Sidious to consolidate power and exterminate the Jedi.

### Order 66 (19 BBY)

Chancellor Palpatine activated a hidden command that turned the clone troopers against their Jedi generals, nearly wiping out the Jedi Order in a single day.

### Battle of Yavin (0 BBY/ABY)

Rebel forces destroyed the first Death Star above Yavin 4. This event anchors the galactic dating system.

### Battle of Endor (4 ABY)

The Rebel Alliance destroyed the second Death Star and Emperor Palpatine appeared to die, ending the Galactic Civil War.

### Battle of Exegol (35 ABY)

The Resistance and a citizen fleet defeated the resurrected Emperor and the Sith Eternal, ending the Skywalker saga.
