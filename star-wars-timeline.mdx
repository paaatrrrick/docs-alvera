## Overview

The Star Wars galaxy spans tens of thousands of years of in-universe history. Most stories are anchored to the Battle of Yavin (the climactic event of *A New Hope*) using two abbreviations:

- **BBY**: Before the Battle of Yavin
- **ABY**: After the Battle of Yavin

## Major eras

### High Republic era (~500-100 BBY)

A golden age of the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic, when Jedi Knights served as guardians of peace across the galaxy. Featured in *The High Republic* publishing initiative and *The Acolyte*.

### Fall of the Jedi (~32-19 BBY)

The events of the prequel trilogy, including the Naboo crisis, the Clone Wars, and Order 66.

- **32 BBY**: Battle of Naboo (*The Phantom Menace*)
- **22 BBY**: Clone Wars begin (*Attack of the Clones*)
- **19 BBY**: Order 66 and the rise of the Empire (*Revenge of the Sith*)

### Reign of the Empire (~19-5 BBY)

The Galactic Empire consolidates power. The Jedi are hunted to near-extinction. Stories from this era include *Solo*, *Obi-Wan Kenobi*, and the early seasons of *Star Wars Rebels*.

### Age of Rebellion (~5 BBY-4 ABY)

The Rebel Alliance forms and fights the Empire. Key events include:

- **5 BBY**: Events of *Andor* and *Rebels* begin
- **0 BBY**: *Rogue One* and the theft of the Death Star plans
- **0 ABY**: Battle of Yavin (*A New Hope*)
- **3 ABY**: Battle of Hoth (*The Empire Strikes Back*)
- **4 ABY**: Battle of Endor (*Return of the Jedi*)

### New Republic era (~5-28 ABY)

The Rebellion becomes the New Republic. The Mandalorian, Grogu, and Ahsoka's adventures take place here, along with Luke Skywalker's attempt to rebuild the Jedi Order.

### Rise of the First Order (~28-35 ABY)

The events of the sequel trilogy, covering the First Order's emergence, the Resistance, and the final defeat of the Sith.
