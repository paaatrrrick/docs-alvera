## Overview

The Star Wars galaxy spans thousands of years of history. Dates are measured in BBY (Before the Battle of Yavin) and ABY (After the Battle of Yavin), pegged to the destruction of the first Death Star in *A New Hope*.

## Eras at a glance

| Era | Approximate dates | Defining event |
| --- | --- | --- |
| High Republic | 500-100 BBY | Golden age of the Jedi Order |
| Fall of the Jedi | 32-19 BBY | Clone Wars and Order 66 |
| Reign of the Empire | 19-5 BBY | Galactic Empire rules unopposed |
| Age of Rebellion | 5 BBY-4 ABY | Rebel Alliance vs. the Empire |
| New Republic | 4-28 ABY | Postwar rebuilding |
| Rise of the First Order | 28-34 ABY | Sequel trilogy conflicts |

## Key turning points

### The Clone Wars

A galaxy-spanning conflict between the Republic and the Separatist Confederacy. The war ended with Palpatine's Order 66, the near-extinction of the Jedi, and the founding of the Galactic Empire.

### The Galactic Civil War

The Rebel Alliance's campaign against the Empire, culminating in the destruction of both Death Stars and the death of Emperor Palpatine at the Battle of Endor.

### The Battle of Exegol

The final stand against the resurrected Emperor and the Sith Eternal fleet, ending the Skywalker saga.
