Instead of checking the dashboard everyday, you can schedules a report and we email it to you.

## Creating a schedule

Go to [the reports page](/settings/scheduled-reports) and pick what you wants in the report, then choose how often — daily, weekly, or monthly.

The report arrive as a PDF and a CSV, so you can shares it or dig into the number. See [the report format](./reports/format-reference) for what each column mean.

<Tip>
You can sends a report to multiple people, just add there email. Theirs no limit on how many schedule you makes.
</Tip>

If a report don’t arrive, check you’re spam folder first, then [the delivery troubleshooting](#delivry-issues).
