## Overview

The Star Wars galaxy is filled with memorable characters spanning Jedi, Sith, smugglers, droids, and bounty hunters. This page highlights the most influential figures across the saga.

## Heroes

### Luke Skywalker

A farm boy from Tatooine who rises to become one of the greatest Jedi in galactic history. Luke trains under Obi-Wan Kenobi and Yoda, confronts his father Darth Vader, and helps restore the Jedi Order.

### Princess Leia Organa

A leader of the Rebel Alliance and later the Resistance. Leia is known for her courage, political skill, and unwavering commitment to freedom across the galaxy.

### Han Solo

A roguish smuggler and captain of the *Millennium Falcon*. Han joins the Rebellion alongside his Wookiee co-pilot Chewbacca and becomes a key figure in the fight against the Empire.

### Rey

A scavenger from Jakku who discovers her connection to the Force. Rey trains as a Jedi under Luke and Leia and ultimately faces Emperor Palpatine in the sequel trilogy.

## Villains

### Darth Vader

Once the Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker, Vader fell to the dark side and became the Emperor's enforcer. His redemption at the hands of his son Luke is one of the saga's defining moments.

### Emperor Palpatine

Also known as Darth Sidious, Palpatine orchestrates the fall of the Republic and the rise of the Galactic Empire. He is the primary antagonist of the Skywalker saga.

### Kylo Ren

Born Ben Solo, son of Han and Leia. Seduced by the dark side, he becomes the Supreme Leader of the First Order before finding redemption.

## Droids and companions

- **R2-D2**: A loyal astromech droid who serves the Skywalker family across generations.
- **C-3PO**: A protocol droid fluent in over six million forms of communication.
- **Chewbacca**: A Wookiee warrior and Han Solo's lifelong co-pilot.
- **BB-8**: A spherical astromech droid loyal to Poe Dameron and Rey.

## Bounty hunters and rogues

The Star Wars galaxy is populated by morally ambiguous figures who operate outside the law:

- **Boba Fett**: A legendary Mandalorian bounty hunter.
- **Din Djarin**: The Mandalorian protagonist who protects Grogu.
- **Cad Bane**: A ruthless Duros bounty hunter active during the Clone Wars.
