## Overview

The Galactic Senate was the primary legislative body of the Galactic Republic. Representatives from thousands of star systems convened on Coruscant to debate policy, ratify treaties, and elect the Supreme Chancellor.

## Structure

The Senate was organized around member systems and sectors, each entitled to representation in the rotunda chamber.

| Role | Responsibility |
| --- | --- |
| Supreme Chancellor | Head of state, presided over Senate sessions |
| Vice Chair | Assisted the Chancellor during proceedings |
| Senator | Represented a planet, sector, or organization |
| Senate Guard | Provided security for senators and the chamber |

## The Senate chamber

The Senate chamber was housed in a massive domed rotunda on Coruscant. Senators addressed the body from repulsorlift platforms that detached from the chamber walls, allowing speakers to float to the center for debate.

## Notable senators

- **Padmé Amidala** — Senator of Naboo and former queen
- **Bail Organa** — Senator of Alderaan and founding member of the Rebel Alliance
- **Mon Mothma** — Senator of Chandrila who later led the Rebellion
- **Palpatine** — Senator of Naboo who rose to Supreme Chancellor and then Emperor

## Decline and dissolution

The Clone Wars granted the Supreme Chancellor sweeping emergency powers. At the end of the conflict, Palpatine reorganized the Republic into the Galactic Empire. The Imperial Senate persisted as a weakened body until Emperor Palpatine officially dissolved it shortly before the Battle of Yavin, transferring authority to regional governors.

## Legacy

After the fall of the Empire, the New Republic established a successor Senate, rotating its capital among member worlds to prevent the concentration of power that had enabled Palpatine's rise.
