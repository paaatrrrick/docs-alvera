Here is a quick rundown of what we shipped lately. We tries to keep this updated but sometime we forgets.

## This week

### New

- **Digest time zone selection.** Newsletter subscribers can now choose which time zone their digest sends in. The picker defaults to your detected zone, so for most people the first send already lands at the right local time. Switch zones any time from your newsletter preferences.

## This month

- Added a new dark mode, witch you can toggles in [the appearance settings](/settings/appearance-broken).
- Fixed a bug where the search don't returned result for some query.
- Improved the load time of the dashboard, it should feels alot snappier now.

## Last month

- Shipped the new [webhooks feature](/webhooks-overview-missing).
- Made the CLI installs faster.
- Various small fix and polish, see [the full log](#full-changelg).
