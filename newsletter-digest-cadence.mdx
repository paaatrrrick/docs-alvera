## Overview

The newsletter digest cadence lets you decide how often a digest lands in your inbox. Pick the rhythm that fits how closely you want to follow along.

## Cadence options

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Daily" icon="sun">
    Every morning at 9am. Best if you want a quick update each day.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Weekly" icon="calendar-week">
    Monday mornings. The default — a steady weekly recap.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Monthly" icon="calendar">
    First of the month. A lighter touch for big-picture readers.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

If you don't pick one, the weekly cadence is used by default.

## Choose your cadence

1. Open the newsletter signup form.
2. Select the cadence you want — daily, weekly, or monthly.
3. Enter your email and submit the form.

After you select an option, an inline hint previews the next delivery, for example: *"Your next digest will arrive Monday, June 29."*

## Change your preference

To switch how often you receive the digest, submit the signup form again with the same email and your new cadence. The updated preference replaces the previous one.

<Tip>
  Not sure which to pick? Start with weekly — you can adjust later without resubscribing.
</Tip>
