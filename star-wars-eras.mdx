## Overview

Star Wars stories span thousands of years of in-universe history. Dates are measured relative to the Battle of Yavin (BBY = Before the Battle of Yavin, ABY = After the Battle of Yavin), the climactic event of *A New Hope*.

## Major eras

### The High Republic (approx. 500-100 BBY)

A golden age of the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic, when the Jedi acted as roving guardians of peace across an expanding frontier.

- Featured in: *The High Republic* publishing initiative, *The Acolyte*
- Key themes: exploration, optimism, the early threat of the Nihil

### Fall of the Jedi (approx. 32-19 BBY)

Covered by the prequel trilogy and *The Clone Wars*, this era follows the slow corruption of the Republic and the manipulation of Chancellor Palpatine.

- Key events: invasion of Naboo, the Clone Wars, Order 66
- Ends with the proclamation of the Galactic Empire

### Reign of the Empire (19-5 BBY)

The Empire consolidates power, hunts surviving Jedi, and builds superweapons. Rebellions are scattered and largely underground.

- Featured in: *Star Wars Rebels*, *Andor*, *Obi-Wan Kenobi*, *Solo*
- Key themes: oppression, resistance, moral compromise

### Age of Rebellion (5 BBY-4 ABY)

The Rebel Alliance unites against the Empire, culminating in the events of the original trilogy.

- Key battles: Scarif, Yavin, Hoth, Endor
- Ends with the death of Emperor Palpatine and the destruction of the second Death Star

### New Republic era (4-28 ABY)

The galaxy attempts to rebuild under the New Republic while Imperial remnants regroup in the Unknown Regions.

- Featured in: *The Mandalorian*, *Ahsoka*, *The Book of Boba Fett*
- Key themes: reconstruction, lingering Imperial threats, the rise of the First Order

### Rise of the First Order (28-35 ABY)

The sequel trilogy era, in which the First Order emerges from the Unknown Regions and the Resistance fights to preserve the New Republic's legacy.

- Key events: destruction of the Hosnian system, Battle of Crait, Battle of Exegol
- Ends with the final defeat of the resurrected Emperor Palpatine

## Timeline at a glance

| Era | Approximate dates | Defining conflict |
| --- | --- | --- |
| High Republic | 500-100 BBY | Jedi vs. the Nihil |
| Fall of the Jedi | 32-19 BBY | Clone Wars |
| Reign of the Empire | 19-5 BBY | Imperial rule vs. early rebels |
| Age of Rebellion | 5 BBY-4 ABY | Galactic Civil War |
| New Republic | 4-28 ABY | Imperial remnants and warlords |
| Rise of the First Order | 28-35 ABY | Resistance vs. First Order |
