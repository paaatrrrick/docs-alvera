## Overview

The Star Wars galaxy spans thousands of years of history, divided into distinct eras that frame the major conflicts, characters, and stories of the franchise. Dates are measured in BBY (Before the Battle of Yavin) and ABY (After the Battle of Yavin), using the destruction of the first Death Star as the reference point.

## Major eras

### The High Republic (approximately 500-100 BBY)

A golden age of the Jedi Order, set centuries before the Skywalker saga. The Jedi serve as guardians of peace across an expanding Republic, facing threats like the Nihil marauders.

### The Fall of the Jedi (32-19 BBY)

Covered by the prequel trilogy. The era begins with the events of *The Phantom Menace* and ends with Order 66 and the rise of the Galactic Empire in *Revenge of the Sith*.

### The Reign of the Empire (19-5 BBY)

The Empire consolidates power across the galaxy. Stories from this era include *Solo*, *Obi-Wan Kenobi*, and the early seasons of *Star Wars Rebels*.

### The Age of Rebellion (5 BBY-4 ABY)

The Rebel Alliance rises to challenge Imperial rule. Includes *Andor*, *Rogue One*, and the original trilogy films *A New Hope*, *The Empire Strikes Back*, and *Return of the Jedi*.

### The New Republic (5-34 ABY)

The post-Empire era of rebuilding. Stories include *The Mandalorian*, *The Book of Boba Fett*, and *Ahsoka*. Remnants of the Empire eventually coalesce into the First Order.

### The Rise of the First Order (34-35 ABY)

The sequel trilogy's primary timeframe. The First Order threatens the New Republic, while Rey, Finn, and Poe lead the Resistance in *The Force Awakens*, *The Last Jedi*, and *The Rise of Skywalker*.
