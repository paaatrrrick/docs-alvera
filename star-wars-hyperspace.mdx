# Hyperspace Travel

Hyperspace is an alternate dimension that allows starships to cross vast distances faster than light.

## Hyperdrives

A ship enters hyperspace using a hyperdrive, following carefully calculated routes to avoid stars, gravity wells, and other hazards.

## Navigation

Accurate jumps depend on up-to-date navicomputer data. A miscalculated route can send a ship straight into a planet or a star.
