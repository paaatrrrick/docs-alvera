# Holocrons

Holocrons are crystalline devices used by both Jedi and Sith to store knowledge. They can only be opened by someone attuned to the corresponding side of the Force.

## Jedi holocrons

Jedi holocrons preserve teachings, histories, and the recorded wisdom of past Masters. They are guarded carefully within the Jedi Archives.

## Sith holocrons

Sith holocrons contain forbidden knowledge and dark side techniques, often guarded by the imprinted consciousness of a long-dead Sith Lord.
