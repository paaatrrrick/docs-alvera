## Overview

Holocrons are crystalline devices that store vast amounts of information, projected through holographic interfaces and guided by a gatekeeper — usually a recording of the Force user who created it. Both the Jedi and the Sith built holocrons to preserve teachings, history, and secrets across generations.

## Jedi holocrons

Jedi holocrons are typically cube-shaped with smooth, geometric facets. They were kept in the Holocron Vault deep within the Jedi Temple on Coruscant.

- **Purpose**: Preserve Jedi philosophy, lightsaber forms, and Force techniques for future generations.
- **Access**: Only those attuned to the light side of the Force can open them.
- **Gatekeepers**: Notable gatekeepers include Master Yoda, Bodo Baas, and Tera Sinube.

## Sith holocrons

Sith holocrons are pyramid-shaped, reflecting the Rule of Two and the focused, aggressive nature of the dark side.

- **Purpose**: Record forbidden knowledge, including Sith alchemy, ancient rituals, and the locations of dark side artifacts.
- **Access**: Require deep connection to the dark side; touching one without preparation can corrupt the holder.
- **Notable holders**: Darth Sidious maintained a private collection, and Darth Bane's holocron survived for a thousand years.

## Notable holocrons

- **Tython holocron**: One of the oldest known Jedi holocrons, containing teachings from the founders of the Order.
- **Telos holocron**: Carried by Jedi Master Atris during the Old Republic era.
- **Sith holocron of Darth Andeddu**: A key artifact sought by Count Dooku and Darth Bane.
- **Great Holocron**: The largest holocron in the Jedi Archives, with knowledge accessible only to Masters of the Council.

## Holocrons in the saga

- During the Clone Wars, Cad Bane was hired to steal a Jedi holocron from the Temple Vault to obtain a list of Force-sensitive children.
- Kanan Jarrus and Ezra Bridger combined a Jedi and Sith holocron on Malachor, gaining a vision of how to defeat the Sith — but also drawing them closer to danger.
- Luke Skywalker spent decades searching for lost holocrons to rebuild the Jedi Order's knowledge after the Empire's purge.
