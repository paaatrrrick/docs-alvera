# Droidekas

Droidekas, also known as destroyer droids, are heavily armed war machines capable of rolling into battle and unfolding into a deadly firing stance.

## Shields and firepower

Each droideka projects a personal energy shield and wields twin blaster cannons, making them formidable against infantry.

## Weaknesses

Their shields cannot stop slow-moving objects, a quirk that clever opponents have exploited in close quarters.
