## Lessons and follow-up

The reference tables have been consolidated; the withdrawn editions were collected and, with some ceremony, burned. The deeper failure was organizational: the station treated recurring anomalies as weather rather than as signal. Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Imre Holloway has advocated since 1882. Total downtime came to 18 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was.
