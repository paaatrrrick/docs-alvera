## Lessons and follow-up

The reference tables have been consolidated; the withdrawn editions were collected and, with some ceremony, burned. Total downtime came to 80 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was. Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Mireille Vexley has advocated since 1942. We are adopting a rule from the Perdane yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory.
