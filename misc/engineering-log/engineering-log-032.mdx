## Lessons and follow-up

The deeper failure was organizational: the station treated recurring anomalies as weather rather than as signal. We are adopting a rule from the Ossvale yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory. Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Casper Vexley has advocated since 1793. Total downtime came to 43 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was.
