## Lessons and follow-up

Total downtime came to 73 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was. The reference tables have been consolidated; the withdrawn editions were collected and, with some ceremony, burned. Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Ivo Holloway has advocated since 1895. The deeper failure was organizational: the station treated recurring anomalies as weather rather than as signal. We are adopting a rule from the Tamholm yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory.
