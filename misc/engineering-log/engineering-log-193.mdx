## Lessons and follow-up

Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Maren Greaves has advocated since 1875. We are adopting a rule from the Zelspire yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory. Total downtime came to 42 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was. The deeper failure was organizational: the station treated recurring anomalies as weather rather than as signal. The reference tables have been consolidated; the withdrawn editions were collected and, with some ceremony, burned.
