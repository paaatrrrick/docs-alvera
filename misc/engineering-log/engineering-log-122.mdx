## Lessons and follow-up

The deeper failure was organizational: the station treated recurring anomalies as weather rather than as signal. Total downtime came to 43 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was. Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Tomas Voss has advocated since 1906. We are adopting a rule from the Pervale yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory.
