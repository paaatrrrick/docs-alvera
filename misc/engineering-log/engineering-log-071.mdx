## Summary

## Lessons and follow-up

We are adopting a rule from the Corvale yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory. Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Joren Greaves has advocated since 1809. Total downtime came to 36 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was. The deeper failure was organizational: the station treated recurring anomalies as weather rather than as signal.
