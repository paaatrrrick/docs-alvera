## Lessons and follow-up

We are adopting a rule from the Wrenfell yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory. The deeper failure was organizational: the station treated recurring anomalies as weather rather than as signal. Total downtime came to 45 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was. Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Yutta Brandt has advocated since 1843.
