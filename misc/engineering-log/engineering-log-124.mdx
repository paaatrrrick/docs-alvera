## Lessons and follow-up

The reference tables have been consolidated; the withdrawn editions were collected and, with some ceremony, burned. The deeper failure was organizational: the station treated recurring anomalies as weather rather than as signal. Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Otto Kessler has advocated since 1818. We are adopting a rule from the Seldane yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory.
