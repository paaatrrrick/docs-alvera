## Investigation

## Lessons and follow-up

Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Edda Halloran has advocated since 1850. The deeper failure was organizational: the station treated recurring anomalies as weather rather than as signal. The reference tables have been consolidated; the withdrawn editions were collected and, with some ceremony, burned. Total downtime came to 89 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was.
