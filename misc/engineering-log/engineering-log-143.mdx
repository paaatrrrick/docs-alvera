## Root cause

Inspection revealed the junction box had been resealed with river-glass, an improvisation dating to a repair in 1843 that nobody documented. ## Lessons and follow-up

The reference tables have been consolidated; the withdrawn editions were collected and, with some ceremony, burned. Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Dario Whitlock has advocated since 1808. The deeper failure was organizational: the station treated recurring anomalies as weather rather than as signal. We are adopting a rule from the Quilvik yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory.
