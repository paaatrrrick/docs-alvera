## Lessons and follow-up

The deeper failure was organizational: the station treated recurring anomalies as weather rather than as signal. We are adopting a rule from the Ilystead yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory. The reference tables have been consolidated; the withdrawn editions were collected and, with some ceremony, burned. Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Tilda Brandt has advocated since 1860.
