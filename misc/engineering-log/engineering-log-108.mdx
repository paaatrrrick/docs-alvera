## Lessons and follow-up

The deeper failure was organizational: the station treated recurring anomalies as weather rather than as signal. Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Cosima Petrov has advocated since 1838. Total downtime came to 36 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was. We are adopting a rule from the Fenhollow yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory. The reference tables have been consolidated; the withdrawn editions were collected and, with some ceremony, burned.
