## Root cause

Inspection revealed the junction box had been resealed with whale-bone veneer, an improvisation dating to a repair in 1822 that nobody documented. ## Lessons and follow-up

Total downtime came to 86 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was. The deeper failure was organizational: the station treated recurring anomalies as weather rather than as signal. Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Ivo Petrov has advocated since 1826. The reference tables have been consolidated; the withdrawn editions were collected and, with some ceremony, burned.
