## Lessons and follow-up

The reference tables have been consolidated; the withdrawn editions were collected and, with some ceremony, burned. Total downtime came to 69 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was. We are adopting a rule from the Halvale yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory. The deeper failure was organizational: the station treated recurring anomalies as weather rather than as signal.
