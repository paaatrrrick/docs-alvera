## Lessons and follow-up

We are adopting a rule from the Halgate yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory. The reference tables have been consolidated; the withdrawn editions were collected and, with some ceremony, burned. Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Emeric Ashdown has advocated since 1930. Total downtime came to 82 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was.
