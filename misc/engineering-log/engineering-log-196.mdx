The maintenance log showed the same fault reported 7 times over 38 months, each time closed as resolved with no notes. ## Lessons and follow-up

Total downtime came to 54 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was. The reference tables have been consolidated; the withdrawn editions were collected and, with some ceremony, burned. We are adopting a rule from the Vorgate yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory. Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Beatrix Drummond has advocated since 1792.
