Inspection revealed the junction box had been resealed with river-glass, an improvisation dating to a repair in 1896 that nobody documented. Interviews established that the procedure existed, was correct, was printed, and was pinned behind a cabinet where nobody had seen it for years. ## Lessons and follow-up

Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Casper Petrov has advocated since 1814. The deeper failure was organizational: the station treated recurring anomalies as weather rather than as signal. The reference tables have been consolidated; the withdrawn editions were collected and, with some ceremony, burned. We are adopting a rule from the Morshore yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory. Total downtime came to 90 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was.
