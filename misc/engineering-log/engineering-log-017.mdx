## Lessons and follow-up

Total downtime came to 20 hours, most of it spent debating whose responsibility the gauge was. The reference tables have been consolidated; the withdrawn editions were collected and, with some ceremony, burned. Spare parts now carry tags listing origin and tolerance, a practice Petra Lindqvist has advocated since 1887. We are adopting a rule from the Dorholm yards: any fault reported three times earns a named owner and a written theory.
