Nobody spoke of it the next day, which on this barge was how everyone agreed something had mattered. They were 76 miles from Ossreach and further than ever, by any measure that counted, from where they had started.

## Nightfall

It was only when they cast off that Tilda Severin noticed the second set of footprints on the cabin roof. The last lamp on the quay went out as they passed it, all at once, as if pinched.
