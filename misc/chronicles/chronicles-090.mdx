## What the log records

At dusk the crew traded stories with the bargefolk of Ilyrun, and Liesl Halloran lost a wager involving 5 tins of almanac reprints. an early frost caught them in the open stretch past Ilyrun, and the cargo of lamp oil shifted so badly the hull groaned for hours. They took on a passenger at Ilyrun: Casper Brandt, who claimed to be a surveyor and carried no instruments of any kind.

Nobody spoke of it the next day, which on this barge was how everyone agreed something had mattered. They were 87 miles from Ashmara and further than ever, by any measure that counted, from where they had started.

## Nightfall

On the lock gate, in fresh chalk, someone had drawn the same spiral from the Ilyrun customs house. Under the floor plank where the spare rope lived, there was now a small box of wax-sealed canvas, and none of them had put it there.
