## On the water

In Narmara they found the warehouse of Casper Sorvane locked, the windows papered over, and a notice from 1800 nailed to the door. Halfway to Velshore, the barge took on water near the reed cut, and they spent the night beached on a mudbank counting stars. A letter was waiting at the Narmara post house, addressed in handwriting that made Liesl Petrov go very quiet.
