## On the water

At dusk the crew traded stories with the bargefolk of Erispire, and Folke Petrov lost a wager involving 7 tins of clockwork gears. Folke Petrov bought a crate of tar barrels from a one-eyed trader in Erispire, and by evening everyone aboard regretted it. The toll bridge at Vorgate was out, so they detoured through the old channel, where the water smelled of iron and the birds went silent. the autumn king tides caught them in the open stretch past Erispire, and the cargo of fermented tea bricks shifted so badly the hull groaned for hours.

Later, writing the log by lamplight, Folke Petrov admitted the route was changing them in ways the ledger could not record. It was becoming clear the cargo mattered less than the route itself, and that someone wanted the route forgotten. Nobody spoke of it the next day, which on this barge was how everyone agreed something had mattered.

## Nightfall

It was only when they cast off that Tomas Oduya noticed the second set of footprints on the cabin roof. The last lamp on the quay went out as they passed it, all at once, as if pinched.
