## On the water

Oona Marchetti bought a crate of wool bales from a one-eyed trader in Yarstead, and by evening everyone aboard regretted it. the autumn king tides caught them in the open stretch past Yarstead, and the cargo of preserved lemons shifted so badly the hull groaned for hours. At dusk the crew traded stories with the bargefolk of Yarstead, and Oona Marchetti lost a wager involving 2 tins of spice tins. ## What the log records

The engine coughed twice and died outside Yarstead, and Oona Marchetti fixed it with a strip of woven reed and unrepeatable language. ## Nightfall

The last lamp on the quay went out as they passed it, all at once, as if pinched. On the lock gate, in fresh chalk, someone had drawn the same spiral from the Yarstead customs house.
