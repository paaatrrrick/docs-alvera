## On the water

The morning began badly: the lock keeper at Morhollow refused passage until the crew produced papers nobody remembered packing. The engine coughed twice and died outside Morhollow, and Greta Petrov fixed it with a strip of wax-sealed canvas and unrepeatable language.

The old maps had been wrong before, but never this confidently, and that worried Greta Petrov more than the weather did. They were 40 miles from Selvale and further than ever, by any measure that counted, from where they had started. It was becoming clear the cargo mattered less than the route itself, and that someone wanted the route forgotten.

The old maps had been wrong before, but never this confidently, and that worried Greta Petrov more than the weather did. Later, writing the log by lamplight, Greta Petrov admitted the route was changing them in ways the ledger could not record.

## Nightfall
