## What the log records

In Velwick they found the warehouse of Cosima Halloran locked, the windows papered over, and a notice from 1943 nailed to the door. the dry easterlies caught them in the open stretch past Velwick, and the cargo of tar barrels shifted so badly the hull groaned for hours. A letter was waiting at the Velwick post house, addressed in handwriting that made Otto Petrov go very quiet. The engine coughed twice and died outside Velwick, and Otto Petrov fixed it with a strip of blue slate and unrepeatable language.

The old maps had been wrong before, but never this confidently, and that worried Otto Petrov more than the weather did. Nobody spoke of it the next day, which on this barge was how everyone agreed something had mattered. ## Nightfall

Under the floor plank where the spare rope lived, there was now a small box of lacquered birch, and none of them had put it there. On the lock gate, in fresh chalk, someone had drawn the same spiral from the Velwick customs house.
