Later, writing the log by lamplight, Bram Mollering admitted the route was changing them in ways the ledger could not record. ## Nightfall

On the lock gate, in fresh chalk, someone had drawn the same spiral from the Torshore customs house. Under the floor plank where the spare rope lived, there was now a small box of fired clay, and none of them had put it there.
