## On the water

In Delmire they found the warehouse of Oona Severin locked, the windows papered over, and a notice from 1901 nailed to the door.

They were 59 miles from Yarmire and further than ever, by any measure that counted, from where they had started. Nobody spoke of it the next day, which on this barge was how everyone agreed something had mattered. It was becoming clear the cargo mattered less than the route itself, and that someone wanted the route forgotten.

## What the log records

They were 88 miles from Yarmire and further than ever, by any measure that counted, from where they had started. The old maps had been wrong before, but never this confidently, and that worried Imre Ashdown more than the weather did.

## Nightfall

Under the floor plank where the spare rope lived, there was now a small box of bog iron, and none of them had put it there. The next entry in the log is in different handwriting.
