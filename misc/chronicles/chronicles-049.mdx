## What the log records

At dusk the crew traded stories with the bargefolk of Delbury, and Imre Oduya lost a wager involving 4 tins of clockwork gears.

Later, writing the log by lamplight, Imre Oduya admitted the route was changing them in ways the ledger could not record. The old maps had been wrong before, but never this confidently, and that worried Imre Oduya more than the weather did. Nobody spoke of it the next day, which on this barge was how everyone agreed something had mattered.

## Nightfall

Under the floor plank where the spare rope lived, there was now a small box of wax-sealed canvas, and none of them had put it there. The next entry in the log is in different handwriting.
