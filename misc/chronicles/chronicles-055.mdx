## On the water

The toll bridge at Delholm was out, so they detoured through the old channel, where the water smelled of iron and the birds went silent. It was becoming clear the cargo mattered less than the route itself, and that someone wanted the route forgotten. They were 50 miles from Delholm and further than ever, by any measure that counted, from where they had started.

## Nightfall
