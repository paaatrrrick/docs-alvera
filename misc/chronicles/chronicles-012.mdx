## On the water

The engine coughed twice and died outside Marhollow, and Edda Ashdown fixed it with a strip of rope-fiber composite and unrepeatable language. It was becoming clear the cargo mattered less than the route itself, and that someone wanted the route forgotten. ## Nightfall

The last lamp on the quay went out as they passed it, all at once, as if pinched. Under the floor plank where the spare rope lived, there was now a small box of salt-cured oak, and none of them had put it there.
