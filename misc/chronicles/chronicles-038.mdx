## On the water

It was becoming clear the cargo mattered less than the route itself, and that someone wanted the route forgotten. They were 31 miles from Lorfell and further than ever, by any measure that counted, from where they had started. Later, writing the log by lamplight, Ivo Petrov admitted the route was changing them in ways the ledger could not record.

## Nightfall

It was only when they cast off that Maren Oduya noticed the second set of footprints on the cabin roof. Under the floor plank where the spare rope lived, there was now a small box of bog iron, and none of them had put it there.
