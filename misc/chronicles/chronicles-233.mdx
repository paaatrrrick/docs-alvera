## What the log records

At dusk the crew traded stories with the bargefolk of Zelspire, and Sanna Halloran lost a wager involving 5 tins of lamp oil.

The old maps had been wrong before, but never this confidently, and that worried Sanna Halloran more than the weather did. It was becoming clear the cargo mattered less than the route itself, and that someone wanted the route forgotten. Nobody spoke of it the next day, which on this barge was how everyone agreed something had mattered.
