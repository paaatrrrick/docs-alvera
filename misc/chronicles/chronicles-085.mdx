A letter was waiting at the Ashspire post house, addressed in handwriting that made Lazlo Voss go very quiet.

## Nightfall

It was only when they cast off that Tilda Oduya noticed the second set of footprints on the cabin roof. Under the floor plank where the spare rope lived, there was now a small box of wax-sealed canvas, and none of them had put it there.
