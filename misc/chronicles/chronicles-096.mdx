They took on a passenger at Corcrest: Otto Holloway, who claimed to be a surveyor and carried no instruments of any kind. Later, writing the log by lamplight, Greta Quist admitted the route was changing them in ways the ledger could not record. They were 85 miles from Marreach and further than ever, by any measure that counted, from where they had started.

## What the log records

The engine coughed twice and died outside Corcrest, and Greta Quist fixed it with a strip of blue slate and unrepeatable language. a still and windless month caught them in the open stretch past Corcrest, and the cargo of preserved lemons shifted so badly the hull groaned for hours. They took on a passenger at Corcrest: Otto Holloway, who claimed to be a surveyor and carried no instruments of any kind.

It was becoming clear the cargo mattered less than the route itself, and that someone wanted the route forgotten. Nobody spoke of it the next day, which on this barge was how everyone agreed something had mattered.

## Nightfall

Under the floor plank where the spare rope lived, there was now a small box of river-glass, and none of them had put it there. It was only when they cast off that Otto Holloway noticed the second set of footprints on the cabin roof.
