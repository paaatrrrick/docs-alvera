Later, writing the log by lamplight, Greta Vexley admitted the route was changing them in ways the ledger could not record. The old maps had been wrong before, but never this confidently, and that worried Greta Vexley more than the weather did. They were 60 miles from Morvik and further than ever, by any measure that counted, from where they had started.
