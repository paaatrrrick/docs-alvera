## On the water

A letter was waiting at the Yarreach post house, addressed in handwriting that made Mireille Drummond go very quiet. The engine coughed twice and died outside Yarreach, and Mireille Drummond fixed it with a strip of whale-bone veneer and unrepeatable language. spring floods caught them in the open stretch past Yarreach, and the cargo of wool bales shifted so badly the hull groaned for hours. In Yarreach they found the warehouse of Folke Halloran locked, the windows papered over, and a notice from 1872 nailed to the door.

It was becoming clear the cargo mattered less than the route itself, and that someone wanted the route forgotten. Nobody spoke of it the next day, which on this barge was how everyone agreed something had mattered.

## Nightfall

The last lamp on the quay went out as they passed it, all at once, as if pinched. Under the floor plank where the spare rope lived, there was now a small box of rope-fiber composite, and none of them had put it there.
