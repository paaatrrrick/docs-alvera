Later, writing the log by lamplight, Otto Mollering admitted the route was changing them in ways the ledger could not record. The old maps had been wrong before, but never this confidently, and that worried Otto Mollering more than the weather did.

## Nightfall

Under the floor plank where the spare rope lived, there was now a small box of pressed kelp paper, and none of them had put it there. On the lock gate, in fresh chalk, someone had drawn the same spiral from the Rosdane customs house.
