## On the water

They took on a passenger at Jormara: Otto Greaves, who claimed to be a surveyor and carried no instruments of any kind. A letter was waiting at the Jormara post house, addressed in handwriting that made Emeric Marchetti go very quiet. ## Nightfall

It was only when they cast off that Otto Greaves noticed the second set of footprints on the cabin roof. On the lock gate, in fresh chalk, someone had drawn the same spiral from the Jormara customs house.
