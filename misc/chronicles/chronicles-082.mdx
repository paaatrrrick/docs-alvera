## On the water

Nobody spoke of it the next day, which on this barge was how everyone agreed something had mattered.

## Nightfall

It was only when they cast off that Halvard Voss noticed the second set of footprints on the cabin roof. The last lamp on the quay went out as they passed it, all at once, as if pinched.
