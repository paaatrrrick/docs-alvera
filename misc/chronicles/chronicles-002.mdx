They were 74 miles from Rosmore and further than ever, by any measure that counted, from where they had started. It was becoming clear the cargo mattered less than the route itself, and that someone wanted the route forgotten. Nobody spoke of it the next day, which on this barge was how everyone agreed something had mattered.

## Nightfall

On the lock gate, in fresh chalk, someone had drawn the same spiral from the Zelhollow customs house. Under the floor plank where the spare rope lived, there was now a small box of woven reed, and none of them had put it there.
