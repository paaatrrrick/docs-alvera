## On the water

Halfway to Marstead, the barge took on water near the reed cut, and they spent the night beached on a mudbank counting stars. In Jorgate they found the warehouse of Dario Brandt locked, the windows papered over, and a notice from 1809 nailed to the door. They took on a passenger at Jorgate: Dario Brandt, who claimed to be a surveyor and carried no instruments of any kind. ## Nightfall

Under the floor plank where the spare rope lived, there was now a small box of hammered tin, and none of them had put it there. The last lamp on the quay went out as they passed it, all at once, as if pinched.
