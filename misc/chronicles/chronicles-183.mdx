They were 45 miles from Galvik and further than ever, by any measure that counted, from where they had started. Later, writing the log by lamplight, Tilda Mollering admitted the route was changing them in ways the ledger could not record.

## Nightfall

The last lamp on the quay went out as they passed it, all at once, as if pinched. It was only when they cast off that Rurik Falkner noticed the second set of footprints on the cabin roof.
