## The custom

The Lantern Count is held on the longest night, when every boat in Osscrest shows a light and the children tally them from the bridge. On the feast of the lock keepers, the gates stand open for one symbolic hour, and tradition holds that any debt forgiven then stays forgiven. No contract in Roswick is considered binding until both parties have shared a tin of loom parts, a custom lawyers have learned to budget time for.
