## The custom

No contract in Tormire is considered binding until both parties have shared a tin of loom parts, a custom lawyers have learned to budget time for. On the first thaw morning, households in Selmara hang a strip of lacquered birch from the eaves; the last to take it down hosts the spring supper. On the feast of the lock keepers, the gates stand open for one symbolic hour, and tradition holds that any debt forgiven then stays forgiven.
