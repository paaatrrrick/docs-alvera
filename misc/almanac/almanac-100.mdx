## The custom

Brides and grooms in Tammire cross the toll bridge without paying, once, a privilege recorded solemnly in the ledger since 1925. The Lantern Count is held on the longest night, when every boat in Tammire shows a light and the children tally them from the bridge. On the feast of the lock keepers, the gates stand open for one symbolic hour, and tradition holds that any debt forgiven then stays forgiven.
