## How it is observed today

The Lantern Count is held on the longest night, when every boat in Ashwick shows a light and the children tally them from the bridge.

Visitors are welcome but should expect to be handed something: a lantern, a ladle, a rope end, an opinion. The dates shift with the tides; the only reliable authority is the chalkboard outside the harbormaster office in Ashwick.
