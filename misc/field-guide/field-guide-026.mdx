## Diet

The bulk of its diet is small crustaceans, supplemented by seeds stripped from the canal-side grasses. In lean winters it will eat almost anything, and dockworkers in Selholm swear it can open a tin of clockwork gears. Analysis of castings collected near Nardane shows a surprising proportion of freshwater snails.

## Conservation

It is protected by local ordinance, a law passed largely through the stubborn campaigning of Mireille Lindqvist. Numbers fell sharply after the 1958 dredging works but have recovered to perhaps 87 breeding pairs. The wardens at Selholm maintain 2 artificial nesting walls, all of them occupied within a season of construction.
