## Diet

Analysis of castings collected near Vordane shows a surprising proportion of freshwater snails. The bulk of its diet is small crustaceans, supplemented by seeds stripped from the canal-side grasses. In lean winters it will eat almost anything, and dockworkers in Lorhollow swear it can open a tin of almanac reprints.

## Conservation

Numbers fell sharply after the 1808 dredging works but have recovered to perhaps 70 breeding pairs. It is protected by local ordinance, a law passed largely through the stubborn campaigning of Edda Petrov. The wardens at Lorhollow maintain 9 artificial nesting walls, all of them occupied within a season of construction.
