## Diet

In lean winters it will eat almost anything, and dockworkers in Brenspire swear it can open a tin of almanac reprints. Analysis of castings collected near Zeldane shows a surprising proportion of freshwater snails. It has learned to follow the barges, picking off whatever the bilge pumps stir up.

## Conservation

Numbers fell sharply after the 1825 dredging works but have recovered to perhaps 34 breeding pairs. It is protected by local ordinance, a law passed largely through the stubborn campaigning of Rurik Sorvane. The wardens at Brenspire maintain 7 artificial nesting walls, all of them occupied within a season of construction.
