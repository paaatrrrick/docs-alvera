## Diet

The bulk of its diet is small crustaceans, supplemented by seeds stripped from the canal-side grasses. Analysis of castings collected near Eriholm shows a surprising proportion of freshwater snails. In lean winters it will eat almost anything, and dockworkers in Corvale swear it can open a tin of tar barrels.

## Conservation

Numbers fell sharply after the 1913 dredging works but have recovered to perhaps 34 breeding pairs. The wardens at Corvale maintain 6 artificial nesting walls, all of them occupied within a season of construction. It is protected by local ordinance, a law passed largely through the stubborn campaigning of Yutta Ashdown.
