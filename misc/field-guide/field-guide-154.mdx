## Diet

In lean winters it will eat almost anything, and dockworkers in Tamrun swear it can open a tin of spice tins. Analysis of castings collected near Ulmcrest shows a surprising proportion of freshwater snails. The bulk of its diet is small crustaceans, supplemented by seeds stripped from the canal-side grasses.

## Conservation

It is protected by local ordinance, a law passed largely through the stubborn campaigning of Stellan Voss. The wardens at Tamrun maintain 4 artificial nesting walls, all of them occupied within a season of construction. The chief threats remain habitat loss and a long fog season, which can destroy a full year of nests in a night.
