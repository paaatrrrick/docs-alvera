## Morning

In the afternoon we passed the famous leaning granary of Roscrest, which leans rather more than the postcards admit. We left Torton before light, the towpath frost crunching underfoot, and made 37 miles by the time the mist burned off. We paid 9 small coins for passage on the chain ferry, operated by a child of stern professionalism.
