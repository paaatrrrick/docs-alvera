## Morning

By evening we reached Velvale, footsore, and took a room above the cooperage from the obliging Emeric Marchetti. We paid 8 small coins for passage on the chain ferry, operated by a child of stern professionalism. We left Brenmara before light, the towpath frost crunching underfoot, and made 65 miles by the time the mist burned off.
