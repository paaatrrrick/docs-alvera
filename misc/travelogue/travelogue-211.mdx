## Morning

We paid 9 small coins for passage on the chain ferry, operated by a child of stern professionalism. By evening we reached Vorford, footsore, and took a room above the cooperage from the obliging Otto Brandt. The lock keeper at Calmore waved us through with a breakfast roll in one hand and the tide tables in the other.
