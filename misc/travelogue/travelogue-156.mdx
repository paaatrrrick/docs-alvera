## Morning

By evening we reached Ashbury, footsore, and took a room above the cooperage from the obliging Maren Halloran. We paid 6 small coins for passage on the chain ferry, operated by a child of stern professionalism. We left Yarton before light, the towpath frost crunching underfoot, and made 14 miles by the time the mist burned off.
