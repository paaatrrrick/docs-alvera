## Morning

By evening we reached Ulmreach, footsore, and took a room above the cooperage from the obliging Yutta Marchetti. We paid 9 small coins for passage on the chain ferry, operated by a child of stern professionalism. Lunch was bread and fermented tea bricks on the wall above the weir, watching barges queue like patient cattle.

## Midday
