## Overview

The Star Wars galaxy spans tens of thousands of years of in-universe history, with shifting governments, warring orders, and evolving conflicts. This page summarizes the most important factions and eras you encounter across the films and series.

## Major factions

### Galactic Republic

The Galactic Republic was the democratic government that ruled the galaxy for a thousand generations, supported by the Jedi Order as peacekeepers. It collapsed at the end of the Clone Wars.

### Galactic Empire

The Empire rose from the ashes of the Republic under Emperor Palpatine and Darth Vader. It centralized power, dissolved the Senate, and ruled through fear, the Imperial Navy, and superweapons like the Death Star.

### Rebel Alliance

The Alliance to Restore the Republic united dissident senators, defectors, and freedom fighters against the Empire. Its victory at Endor led to the founding of the New Republic.

### First Order and Resistance

Decades after the Empire's fall, remnants regrouped as the First Order. The Resistance, led by General Leia Organa, opposed them throughout the sequel trilogy.

## Historical eras

- **High Republic (approx. 500-100 BBY)**: A golden age of exploration and Jedi influence.
- **Fall of the Jedi (32-19 BBY)**: The prequel trilogy and Clone Wars.
- **Reign of the Empire (19-5 BBY)**: *Solo*, *Andor*, *Obi-Wan Kenobi*, and *Rogue One*.
- **Age of Rebellion (5-0 BBY)**: The original trilogy era.
- **New Republic (4-28 ABY)**: *The Mandalorian*, *Ahsoka*, *The Book of Boba Fett*.
- **Rise of the First Order (28-35 ABY)**: The sequel trilogy.

## Iconic starships

- **Millennium Falcon**: Han Solo's modified YT-1300 light freighter.
- **X-wing starfighter**: Versatile Rebel and Resistance fighter.
- **TIE fighter**: Mass-produced Imperial short-range fighter.
- **Star Destroyer**: Wedge-shaped capital ship and symbol of Imperial might.
