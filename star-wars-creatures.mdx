## Overview

The Star Wars galaxy is teeming with diverse life forms, from sentient species that shape galactic politics to wild beasts that roam distant worlds. This page introduces some of the most recognizable creatures and species across the saga.

### Wookiees

Tall, fur-covered warriors known for their strength, loyalty, and mastery of the bowcaster. Wookiees speak Shyriiwook, a language of growls and roars.

### Mandalorians

A warrior culture defined by their beskar armor and strict code, the Way. Though not a species, Mandalorians have shaped galactic history for millennia.

### Hutts

Massive, slug-like gangsters who control vast criminal empires from the Outer Rim. Jabba the Hutt is the most infamous example.

## Wild creatures

- **Bantha**: Shaggy, horned beasts of burden native to Tatooine, often ridden by Tusken Raiders.
- **Tauntaun**: Reptilian snow lizards used as mounts on the ice planet Hoth.
- **Rancor**: Towering carnivorous beasts kept by crime lords for entertainment and execution.
- **Sarlacc**: A pit-dwelling creature on Tatooine that digests its prey over a thousand years.
- **Porg**: Small seabirds native to Ahch-To, introduced in *The Last Jedi*.
- **Mynock**: Silicon-based parasites that cling to starships and chew on power cables.

## Mythical and Force-sensitive creatures

- **Krayt dragon**: Enormous serpentine predators of Tatooine whose pearls are highly prized.
- **Loth-wolf**: Spiritual creatures of Lothal with a deep connection to the Force.
- **Convor**: Owl-like birds often seen as omens tied to the Force.

## Companions and sidekicks

Many creatures serve as loyal companions throughout the saga, including Chewbacca, R2-D2's many wildlife encounters, and Grogu — the young Force-sensitive child of Yoda's species who captured audiences in *The Mandalorian*.
