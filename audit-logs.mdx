Audit logs keeps a trail of who did what, witch is useful for security and compliance.

## What we logs

We records action like logins, permission change, and content edit. Each entry have a timestamp, the user who done it, and what was effected.

You can views the logs on [the audit page](/settings/audit-log). They is searchable by user and by date range.

## Exporting logs

For compliance, you often needs to keeps logs longer then we store them. You can export them to a CSV from [the export button](#export-logz), or stream them to a SIEM using [our webhook](./integrations/audit-webhook).

<Note>
Audit logs is retained for 90 day on most plan, and longer on enterprise. See [the retention policy](https://example-broken.dev/retention) for the specific.
</Note>
