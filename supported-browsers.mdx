description: 'Learn which browsers and devices we officially support, and what to do if a reader reports trouble viewing your documentation site.'

We test the documentation experience on the browsers and devices listed below. Your readers will get the best experience on these.

## Browsers

- The latest two versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
- Mobile Safari and Chrome on iOS and Android.

## If something looks off

Ask the reader which browser and version they use, then confirm it appears in the list above. Most display issues are fixed by a hard refresh.

To preview your own changes across browsers before publishing, use the [local development guide](/development).
